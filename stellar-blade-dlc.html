Stellar Blade DLC: Raven Returns - All Content and Release Details

Sau man ra mat gay chan dong nam 2024, Stellar Blade tiep tuc voi DLC mo rong — Shift Up chung to ho khong chi la studio mot lan.

Stellar Blade's new DLC opens Raven's story, the fan-favourite character since launch, with a gravity mechanic that immediately rewrites how combat feels.

Stellar Blade DLC phat hanh 2025 tren PS5 va PC. Noi dung: chuong moi mo rong lore, trang phuc Eve moi, boss tu Eidos 7. Shift Up xac nhan dang phat trien Stellar Blade 2.
Raven Returns DLC launches July 18, 2026, priced $19.99. New region: Altea Basin. New mechanic: Gravity Core. Playable characters: both Eve and Raven (for the first time). 5-6 hours of main content.

Stellar Blade la cau chuyen thanh cong bat ngo cua nam 2024 — mot studio Han Quoc chua tung lam game console truoc do tao ra mot trong nhung action RPG hay nhat cua the he PS5. DLC khong chi la noi dung them vao; voi Shift Up, day la co hoi de chung minh ho co the duy tri chat luong do.

Chuong Moi: Eidos 7 va Bi Mat Bi Chon Vui

DLC mo rong vao giai doan truoc su kien chinh cua game — tai Eidos 7, mot trong nhung co so nghien cuu bi huy diet som nhat trong cuoc xam lang. Eve va dong doi kham pha nhung gi thuc su xay ra tai day, va trong qua trinh do he lo them ve nguon goc cua Naytibas — nhung ke thu sinh co hoc da chiem dong Trai Dat. Lore mo rong nay duoc nhieu fan coi la phan hap dan nhat DLC.

Boss Moi: Thu Thach Danh Cho Nhung Nguoi Thuc Su Gioi

Stellar Blade noi tieng voi he thong parry-based combat doi hoi ky nang cao. DLC tiep tuc truyen thong do voi ba boss moi, moi boss co attack pattern hoan toan khac voi game goc va yeu cau nguoi choi thuc su hieu thau co che chien dau. Boss cuoi cua DLC dac biet — nhieu nguoi cho rằng no kho ngang ngua voi Gigas trong game goc, nhung satisfying hon nhieu khi vuot qua.

Trang Phuc Moi: Shift Up Biet Khan Gia Cua Ho

Mot phan khong the thieu cua Stellar Blade la he thong trang phuc cho Eve — va DLC bo sung hon 15 outfit moi, bao gom nhung thiet ke lay cam hung tu Blade Runner, cyberpunk Tokyo va tham chi mot so crossover voi cac IP khac cua Shift Up. Day la phan fan service duoc thuc hien mot cach thong minh: trang phuc anh huong den cach moi truong phan ung voi Eve trong mot so canh.

PC Port va Tuong Lai Stellar Blade 2

Cung luc voi DLC, Shift Up phat hanh Stellar Blade cho PC (Steam) voi ho tro day du ray tracing, DLSS 4, va Frame Generation. Quan trong hon, CEO Hyung-Tae Kim xac nhan trong phong van thang 4/2025 rằng Stellar Blade 2 dang trong giai doan concept — "chung toi co nhieu cau chuyen muon ke hon ve the gioi nay."

Danh Gia DLC: 8.0/10 — Xung Dang Cho Fan

Stellar Blade DLC khong cach mang hoa trai nghiem, nhung no lam dung nhung gi mot DLC tot nen lam: mo rong lore mot cach co y nghia, thach thuc nguoi choi quen thuoc voi game goc, va dat nen tang cho tuong lai cua franchise. 8.0/10 phan anh mot goi noi dung solid tu studio dang truong thanh nhanh chong.

When Stellar Blade launched in early 2024, Raven was the character the community talked about most: mysterious, powerful and with a personal arc deeper than any trailer had revealed. Shift Up listened. Raven Returns, the game's first major DLC, officially lands July 18, 2026 and puts Raven at the center of the story in exactly the way fans hoped.

Altea Basin: Unexplored Territory

The new map Altea Basin is a mid-elevation zone blending post-apocalyptic architecture with thick tropical forest, where the largest Alpha Naytiba in the game reside. Shift Up designed Altea Basin as an edge of Stellar Blade's world, where Raven's past and Colony's plans are intimately connected. There are roughly 8 sub-areas, 3 side dungeons and a main dungeon with its own final boss. Exploration pace is slower and more deliberate than Xion, fitting the DLC's contemplative narrative tone.

Gravity Core: A New Combat Mechanic

This is the DLC's biggest gameplay change. The Gravity Core is Raven's secondary weapon, a device generating a localized gravitational field that can pull enemies close, push them away or pin them in place for about 2 seconds. Combined with Eve's existing weapon arsenal, this creates entirely new combo chains: pull an enemy with Gravity Core, land a Parry strike, then finish with a Beta Energy discharge. Shift Up confirms DLC bosses are specifically designed to counter and interact with Gravity Core, so players cannot simply rely on old habits.

Playable Raven: What Fans Wanted From the Start

For the first time in the game's history, Raven is directly playable. In the DLC, players will control Raven during certain flash-memory sequences to understand her past and her relationship with the Colony. Raven has a distinct moveset from Eve: weighted toward speed and punish windows rather than parry timing, with a secret signature finisher Shift Up has not fully revealed. Playing as Raven is described as "offensive-first" where Eve is "reactive-first".

New Outfits and Cosmetic Content

The DLC includes 4 new outfits for Eve and 2 for Raven, including an "Altea Ranger" survival-style set and an official Colony uniform. Additionally, Shift Up includes 12 new Cosmetic Drone skins, 3 new weapon skins and a Photo Mode update with new filters. All cosmetic content within the DLC is not behind an additional paywall: everything is included in the $19.99 package.

Raven is the character we wanted to tell more about before the main game even launched. This DLC is our opportunity to clearly show what really happened before Eve's arrival. Kim Hyung Tae, CEO Shift Up

Release Date and Pricing

Stellar Blade DLC: Raven Returns launches July 18, 2026 on PS5, priced at $19.99. Complete Edition owners (base game + season pass) receive the DLC at no additional cost. Shift Up confirms this is the first of two planned DLCs for Stellar Blade, with the second having no confirmed title or date yet.
