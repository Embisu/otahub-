Top 10 Best Anime of 2025: Looking Back at an Exceptional Year

OtaHub's Top 10 anime 2025 based on: production quality, narrative value, cultural impact and rewatchability. Number 1: Dungeon Meshi. Must not miss: Dan Da Dan and Frieren.

Moi nam deu co nguoi tuyen bo do la "nam tot nhat cua anime" — nhung 2025 thuc su xung dang voi danh hieu do. Khong phai vi so luong anime, ma vi mat do cua nhung tua xuat sac: hiem co nam nao top 5 deu dang diem tren 8.5 nhu the nay.

1. Solo Leveling Mua 2 — Dinh Cao Cua Anime Webtoon

A-1 Pictures tiep tuc lam dieu khong tuong: bien mot manhwa webtoon thanh anime voi chat luong cinema. Arc Jeju Island la anime sequence hay nhat nam 2025, va su xuat hien cua cac Monarch mo ra quy mo hoan toan moi. 9.0/10. Ly do xung dang so 1: khong co diem yeu dang ke nao trong toan bo 13 tap.

2. Jujutsu Kaisen — Man Chao Tu Biet Hoan Hao

Arc cuoi cua JJK ket thuc manga voi nhung episode anime dat chat luong animation dinh cao nhat MAPPA tung thuc hien. Du ket thuc manga con gay tranh cai, anime adaptation cua arc Sukuna final la khong the che. 8.9/10.

3. Dragon Ball DAIMA — Di San Cua Toriyama

Tua anime cuoi cung duoi su giam sat truc tiep cua Akira Toriyama truoc khi ong mat. DAIMA khong phai la Dragon Ball hoan hao, nhung la Dragon Ball tot nhat ke tu nhung nam dinh cao cua Z. 8.2/10.

4. Mushoku Tensei Mua 3 — Isekai Nghiem Tuc Nhat

Studio Bind tiep tuc giu vung chat luong voi arc Rudeus truong thanh ve ca suc manh lan nhan cach. Mushoku Tensei van la benchmark cua isekai nghiem tuc, va mua 3 la phan hay nhat cho den nay. 8.8/10.

5. Vinland Saga Mua 3 — Cung Duong Hoa Binh Tiep Tuc

Wit Studio tiep tuc chuyen the Vinland Saga voi su ton trong tuyet doi danh cho nguyen tac. Hanh trinh cua Thorfinn tim kiem vung dat khong co chien tranh van la mot trong nhung cau chuyen hay nhat manga ve triet hoc va con nguoi. 9.1/10.

6-10: Nhung Bat Ngo Va On Dinh

Vi tri 6 den 10: Blue Box (romance nhe nhang nhung executed hoan hao — 8.5/10), Dandadan S2 (comedy-action ky quai tiep tuc lam tot — 8.3/10), Bleach TYBW Part Final (ket thuc xung dang — 8.4/10), Frieren S2 (slice-of-life isekai cam xuc nhat nam — 9.0/10), Oshi no Ko S2 (nganh giai tri dark nhat anime — 8.6/10).

Ket Luan: 2025 — Nam Anime Vi Dai

Neu phai chon chu de xuyen suot top anime 2025, do la: chat luong animation dang tien hoa nhanh hon bao gio het. Ca A-1 Pictures, MAPPA, Wit va Bind deu day gioi han ky thuat va nghe thuat trong nam 2025. Day khong phai ngau nhien — day la ket qua cua investment va competition giua cac studio dang ngay cang tang cao.

2025 was a year where even casual anime viewers had to admit: there is too much to watch and too little time. From Trigger delivering the year's most consistent production quality, Science SARU erupting to production heights, to sequels faithfully honoring their source manga. OtaHub looks back at the 10 most outstanding anime of the year so you have a watchlist in case you missed any.

1. Dungeon Meshi (Delicious in Dungeon)

There is nothing to debate here. Trigger's Dungeon Meshi is the anime of 2025 by any criterion you choose: consistent animation quality, intricately built world, characters with genuine depth and a completely unique take on fantasy gastronomy unlike anything that came before. A series capable of delivering both gentle warmth and devastating tragedy within the same episode deserves to be called exceptional. Watch it if you have not, rewatch it if you have.

2. Dan Da Dan

Science SARU and Netflix showed the world what they can do when given creative freedom. Dan Da Dan is the hardest series of the year to summarize: teenage romance, extraterrestrial monsters, ugly spirits and chapters drawn from Japanese urban legends all blend together in a way only Tatsuya Matsumoto could conceive. A completely unique animation style and an EDM-meets-folk soundtrack unlike anything on the market.

3. Frieren: Beyond Journey's End Part 1 Conclusion

Frieren is the first anime in history that genuinely made "nothing happening" compelling. Part 1 closed with the First Class Exam arc, and its final episodes clarified why Frieren values the mages she meets longer than any title: people are the last thing remaining after everything else is gone.

4. Kaiju No. 8

Production I.G accomplished what many thought would fail: adapting a shonen manga about kaiju with a middle-aged protagonist into a 12-episode anime while retaining its energy. Kaiju No. 8 is engaging from episode one to the finale, with action sequences shot in a shoulder-camera style that makes the market's destruction feel less heroic commercial and more genuinely terrifying.

5. Oshi no Ko Season 2

Season 2 of Oshi no Ko is where the series confirmed it is more than an "idol anime." The 2.5D Stage Play arc in Season 2 is a sharp critique of performance art, personal memoirs and how the entertainment industry manipulates both. Doga Kobo adapted it better than manga fans expected.

6. Wind Breaker

The year's biggest surprise breakout. Wind Breaker, the motorcycle gang youth manga by Kohei Horikoshi (My Hero Academia author), was adapted by CloverWorks with obvious passion and race animation full of kinetic energy. Standout feature: the series has independently capable antagonist characters superior to most contemporary shonen.

7. Sengoku Youko

Sengoku Youko is the year's hidden find, an older manga by Satoshi Mizukami finally receiving an adaptation that confirmed why Mizukami is the "best-kept secret" of 2000s manga. Studio Lapin Track handled it with the delicacy the source material deserved.

8. Astro Note

An original anime from Studio Deen and Yostar promising: Astro Note is a light, extremely easy-to-watch sci-fi rom-com at 12 episodes with clean protagonist chemistry. Not the anime of the year, but the one to watch when you need to decompress.

9. Alya Sometimes Hides Her Feelings in Russian

The most-watched school rom-com anime of Summer 2025. Alya-san is smart and composed on the surface but loudly expresses genuine feelings in Russian. Stable animation and outstanding main couple chemistry.

10. Mushoku Tensei Season 2 Part 2

Mushoku Tensei continues doing what it does best: extended coming-of-age narrative, detailed world-building and a protagonist learning from his mistakes in a way rare in isekai. Studio Bind maintained consistent high quality from Season 1.
