Blue Protocol Review: Does the Most Beautiful Anime MMORPG of 2026 Live Up to the Hype?

200 gio, 5 class, 1 raid clear va nhieu lan crash server sau: day la danh gia that su cua Blue Protocol.

200 hours, 5 classes, 1 raid clear and multiple server crashes later: here is our honest take on Blue Protocol.

Summary
Diem so OtaHub: 7.8/10. Diem manh: do hoa anime dep nhat thi truong, combat fluid, world building tot. Diem yeu: server on dinh kem, endgame mong sau launch, monetization hang co cua. F2P-friendly o noi dung chinh.
OtaHub Score: 7.8/10. Strengths: best anime visuals on the market, fluid combat, good world building. Weaknesses: poor server stability, thin endgame post-launch, pay-gated cosmetics. F2P-friendly for main content.

Ba lan delay, hai closed beta va mot lan doi ten sau khi Amazon Games rut lui: Blue Protocol cuoi cung cung co mat o thi truong global vao dau thang 5 nam 2026 duoi su phat hanh cua Bandai Namco Online truc tiep. Day la tua game duoc chat vay boi cong dong anime-gaming nhieu nam, va sau 200 gio choi thu nghiem, OtaHub co the noi: no dang de choi hon ban nghi, nhung cung gian nan hon ban muon.

Do Hoa: Dinh Cua Thi Truong

Diem khong the tranh cai: Blue Protocol co do hoa anime dep nhat trong bat ky MMORPG nao hien tai. Shader cua Bandai Namco tai hien kha nang mau sac cua cel-shading anime len tam muc cong nghiep, voi anh sang dong, hieu ung thoi tiet va particle effect tren combat skills deu tao cam giac xem mot tap anime co ngan sach lon. Thanh pho Asterliese va khu vuc Triadimension Gold Forest la hai diem dep nhat game, noi do hoa va am nhac ket hop tao cam giac song dong that su.

Combat: Fluid Nhung Can Depth

He thong chien dau cua Blue Protocol la action-based voi 5 class: Blade Warden, Foe Breaker, Keen Strider, Spell Weaver va Twin Striker. Moi class co feel rieng biet va chain combo rieng: Blade Warden co rhythmic parry system, Twin Striker co buff management phuc tap nhat. Sau 200 gio, combat van con thu vi, dac biet la o boss fights level cao noi boss co nhieu phase va mechanic. Tuy nhien, so voi cac ARPG don choi nhu Elden Ring hay Devil May Cry, do sau cua tung move set van con chat, dac biet o cac class long-range nhu Keen Strider.

Story: Bui Mat Nhung De Di

Truyen cua Blue Protocol khong phai ly do ban o lai, nhung no cung khong kho chiu den muc ban muon skip. Cau chuyen chau lu chao do Tempest, nhung Bringer (nhan vat tu tuong lai hoac qua khu) va su tranh chap giua Borderlands Guilds la khung narrative du de ban cam thay co muc tieu khi len level. Story quest duoc lam bai ban hon nhieu so voi open beta, co dub tieng Nhat chuan chat luong va CG cutscene dep. Su co lon la ket chuyen chinh campaign qua nganh, het khoang 40-50 gio la xong phan mainline.

Endgame: Mong o Thoi Diem Nay

Day la diem yeu lon nhat cua Blue Protocol luc launch. Endgame hien tai bao gom: mot raid 8-player (Dawnwatcher Sanctum), hai loai daily dungeon, weekly challenges va mot he thong craft rang buoc kha phuc tap. Dawnwatcher Sanctum co design tot va yeu cau coordination cao, nhung chi co mot raid sau launch la khong du cho hardcore player. So sanh voi Final Fantasy XIV hay Lost Ark o thoi diem launch, Blue Protocol thieu ro rang noi dung long-term. Bandai Namco hua them raid va mode PvP trong vong 6 thang dau.

Monetization: Dep Nhung Co Dat

Blue Protocol la game free-to-play, va khong co pay-to-win theo dung nghia den: tat ca noi dung chien dau deu kien nhan duoc. Tuy nhien, he thong Battle Pass va Cosmetic Gacha moi chinh la van de. Mot bo trang phuc full se ton khoang 30-40 USD khi mua rieng, va nhieu skin dep nhat bi nhet sau gacha premium. Gacha khong anh huong den suc manh nhan vat, nhung rate cua paid cosmetics kha thap. F2P player se co du skin de choi vui nhung se nhin thay nguoi khac dep hon ro rang.

Blue Protocol la MMORPG dep nhat toi tung choi. Nhung dep khong du de giu toi neu endgame khong tang cuong sau lan update dau. OtaHub Reviewer, sau 200 gio choi

Ket Luan: 7.8/10

Blue Protocol la dieu hiem thay: mot MMORPG anime thuc su duoc lam tot ve mat mi thuat va co feel chien dau thu vi. No xung dang duoc thu neu ban yeu thich aesthetic anime va ARPG. Nhung o thoi diem launch, endgame mong va server hay den gia do chua du de giu nhung nguoi choi nghiem tuc. Neu Bandai Namco dua ra update endgame tot trong quy 3, Blue Protocol co tiem nang lon. Den thoi diem hien tai, no la tua game de thu, kho giu.

Three delays, two closed betas and one name change after Amazon Games withdrew: Blue Protocol finally reached the global market in early May 2026 under direct Bandai Namco Online publishing. This has been one of the most anticipated anime-gaming titles for years, and after 200 hours of testing, OtaHub can say: it is more playable than you expected, and more frustrating than you wanted.

Visuals: Best in Class

The undeniable highlight: Blue Protocol has the best anime visuals of any current MMORPG. Bandai Namco's shader technology renders cel-shading at a production level, with dynamic lighting, weather effects and combat skill particle effects all creating the feel of watching a high-budget anime episode. The city of Asterliese and the Triadimension Gold Forest region are the two most beautiful areas, where visuals and music combine to create a genuinely alive feeling.

Combat: Fluid but Needs Depth

Blue Protocol's combat is action-based with 5 classes: Blade Warden, Foe Breaker, Keen Strider, Spell Weaver and Twin Striker. Each class has a distinct feel and unique chain combo system: Blade Warden features a rhythmic parry system, Twin Striker has the most complex buff management. After 200 hours, combat remains engaging, particularly in high-level boss fights with multiple phases and mechanics. However, compared to single-player ARPGs like Elden Ring or Devil May Cry, each class's individual moveset depth remains shallow, especially for long-range classes like Keen Strider.

Story: Palatable but Thin

Blue Protocol's narrative is not why you will stay, but it is not unpleasant enough to skip either. The story of a world thrown into chaos by the Tempest, the Bringers who arrive from other times, and the conflict between Borderlands Guilds provides enough of a framework to feel motivated while leveling. Story quests are notably more polished than in the open beta, with quality Japanese voice acting and beautiful CG cutscenes. The major downside is that the main campaign wraps up quickly, finishing around 40-50 hours.

Endgame: Thin at Launch

This is Blue Protocol's biggest weakness at launch. Current endgame includes: one 8-player raid (Dawnwatcher Sanctum), two types of daily dungeons, weekly challenges and a fairly complex crafting system. Dawnwatcher Sanctum is well-designed and requires high coordination, but having only one raid at launch is insufficient for hardcore players. Compared to Final Fantasy XIV or Lost Ark at their respective launches, Blue Protocol clearly lacks long-term content. Bandai Namco has promised additional raids and a PvP mode within the first six months.

Monetization: Beautiful but Pricey

Blue Protocol is free-to-play, with no pay-to-win in the true sense: all combat content can be earned through patience. However, the Battle Pass and Cosmetic Gacha system are where the issues lie. A full outfit set costs around $30-40 when purchased individually, and the best-looking skins are locked behind premium gacha. The gacha does not affect character power, but paid cosmetic rates are fairly low. F2P players will have enough skins to enjoy the game but will visibly notice others looking more impressive.

Blue Protocol is the most beautiful MMORPG I have ever played. But beauty alone will not keep me here if the endgame does not improve after the first major update. OtaHub Reviewer, after 200 hours of play

Verdict: 7.8/10

Blue Protocol is a rare thing: an anime MMORPG genuinely well-crafted in terms of aesthetics and with genuinely enjoyable combat feel. It is worth trying if you love anime aesthetics and ARPGs. But at launch, thin endgame and unreliable servers are not yet enough to retain serious players. If Bandai Namco delivers strong endgame updates in Q3, Blue Protocol has significant potential. Right now it is an easy game to start and a hard game to stay with.
