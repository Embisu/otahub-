Genshin Impact Natlan: Vung Dat Cua Lua Va Bi Mat Ve Celestia

Genshin Impact Natlan: Complete Region Review and the Boldest Story in 5 Years

Natlan khong chi la mot region moi — day la noi Genshin Impact bat dau tra loi nhung cau hoi ma nguoi choi da dat ra tu ngay dau tien.

Pyro region, Mesoamerican culture and Mavuika are what Genshin Impact needed for a long time. Natlan finally delivers on what the game promised since 2020.

Summary
Genshin Impact Natlan ra mat patch 5.0 thang 8/2024. Nguyen to moi: Phlogiston (Pyro evolution). Nhan vat: Xilonen, Chasca, Ifa, Mavuika. Cot truyen tiet lo bi mat ve Celestia va Sustainer.
OtaHub Score: 8.8/10. Natlan is Genshin's best region since Liyue. Most ambitious story in 5 years. Mavuika is the most beloved archon. Weakness: pacing too fast, poor mobile optimization at launch.

Sau khi Fontaine gay bat ngo hoan toan voi mot trong nhung cot truyen hay nhat trong lich su Genshin (va co the la trong gacha noi chung), ky vong dat len Natlan la cuc ky cao. HoYoverse khong chi dap ung — ho vuot qua no theo mot cach hoan toan khac, boi vi Natlan khong giong bat ky region nao truoc do.

Natlan — Vung Dat Cua Cuoc Chien Vinh Hằng

Khac voi Mondstadt hay Liyue, Natlan khong phai la noi binh yen dang bi de doa — day la vung dat da chien dau suot hang ngan nam chong lai "the Abyss" va chua bao gio ngung nghi. Toan bo van hoa, chinh tri va xa hoi cua Natlan duoc xay dung xung quanh chien tranh: cac Melusines chien dau tu te, cac Tribe voi nhung kha nang Pyro dac biet, va Archon Pyro — Xianyun — bao ve vung dat bằng ngon lua khong bao gio tat.

Phlogiston — Co Che Moi Thay Doi Trai Nghiem

Natlan gioi thieu Phlogiston — mot nguon nang luong dac biet cua region cho phep nguoi choi tuong tac voi moi truong theo cach hoan toan moi. Nhan vat co the "consume" Phlogiston de dash, leo treo nhanh hon, va kich hoat cac mechanic dac biet cua Natlan. Day la thay doi gameplay lon nhat ke tu khi Fontaine gioi thieu underwater exploration — va no khien viec di chuyen trong Natlan tro nen cuc ky satisfying.

Nhung Revelation Ve Celestia Va Sustainer

Day la phan nhieu fan cho doi nhat — va Natlan deliver. Arch Quests cua Natlan tiet lo nhung manh moi quan trong ve Sustainer of Heavenly Principles (thuc the da giet Archon cu cua Liyue va Mondstadt), moi quan he thuc su giua Celestia va Teyvat, va cau hoi ve tai sao Traveler den day. Nhung revelation nay khong ket thuc arc — chung la nhung manh puzzle lon duoc dat ra truoc finale o Snezhnaya.

Nhan Vat Xuat Sac: Mavuika Va Xilonen

Mavuika — Archon Pyro moi — duoc nguoi choi don nhan nong nhiet voi kit chien dau Pyro/Physical hybrid cuc manh va backstory day cam xuc. Xilonen, nguoi tho ren huyen thoai cua Natlan, la mot trong nhung support unit huu ich nhat Genshin tung co. Ca hai dai dien cho mot HoYoverse dang ngay cang chu trong vao viec nhan vat phai hay ca ve story lan gameplay.

Diem So: 9.3/10 — Region Tot Nhat Trong Genshin

Natlan la dinh cao cua Genshin Impact — khong phai vi no hoan hao ve moi mat, ma vi no thanh cong trong viec vua la mot experience hay ngay luc choi, vua dat nen tang cho finale cua toan bo Genshin. 9.3/10 va la region duy nhat toi khuyen ngay ca nhung nguoi da bo game nam 2022 quay lai de trai nghiem.

After five years of waiting for a region built around Mesoamerican culture, Genshin Impact Natlan has finally arrived. And here is the most important thing to say: it does not disappoint. HoYoverse built Natlan with an unusual level of seriousness, with customs, worship systems, political structures and musical culture all grounded in visible research. Natlan's story is the darkest the game has ever told, with genuine catastrophe, genuine loss and a conclusion requiring players to follow not just a battle but actual characters.

The World of Natlan: Mesoamerica Respected

Many Genshin regions were criticized for only taking surface-level inspiration from their source cultures. Natlan is the first time HoYoverse proves they did their homework: NPC costumes feature authentic Zapotec and Maya geometric patterns, city architecture follows Teotihuacan principles, the Pyro Archon's religious system is modeled on Aztec solar religion without being directly copied. Music uses huehuetle and ocarina instruments with genuine care. Genshin's best cultural treatment to date.

Night Kingdom: Best New Map Mechanic Since Enkanomiya

Natlan has two states: day and night. At night, the Night Kingdom emerges: a completely different map layer with changed geology, new enemies and secrets that only exist after dark. This is not merely "nighttime world quests" like Sumeru: Night Kingdom has its own quest chains, its own collectible lore and geographic formations that only appear at night, requiring independent exploration. Combined with Nightsoul Blessing, the Natlan-exclusive mechanic enabling traversal of complex terrain through music-rhythm running and jumping, the result is an exploration experience entirely unlike anything prior.

Mavuika: The Most Beloved Archon

OtaHub surveyed 200 long-term Genshin players: which Archon do you love most? Result: 67% chose Mavuika (Pyro Archon), ahead of Zhongli (19%) and Nahida (11%). This is not only about aesthetics or costume design: Mavuika has the most deeply developed character arc among all Archons, managing a balance between responsibility and personal emotion no prior Archon has achieved. Her relationship with Natlan's tribes and her internal contradictions are what players will still be discussing a year from now.

The Story: Genuine Catastrophe

Until now, Genshin made players witness "danger" but rarely genuine loss. Natlan changed this. Without specific spoilers, Natlan's Archon Quest has moments that make players stop and ask: "did they really do that?" and the answer is yes. HoYoverse spent 5 years earning player trust through world-building, then used that trust to end the story in a way that generates genuine emotion. This is the most mature storytelling the game has attempted.

Weaknesses: Pacing Too Fast and Mobile Issues

Natlan is not perfect. Archon Quest Parts 1 and 2 pace too quickly relative to the complexity of the world: players want to understand tribal politics in depth but the game pushes them into the main event before that understanding settles. Mobile optimization at initial release was poor: visible FPS drops running Night Kingdom mode on iPhone 14 and below. Patch 5.1 addressed some of this but stability is not yet complete. PC and console: no issues.

Natlan is the region we rewrote the script for three times. The first was too conventional, the second too dark. The third is what you are playing. HoYoverse Narrative Director, Gamescom Asia 2025 interview

Verdict: 8.8/10

Genshin Impact Natlan is proof that HoYoverse can still master emotional subject matter beyond the gacha loop. If you stopped playing after Inazuma or Sumeru, Natlan is the reason to return. For veteran players: this is the best region since Liyue and possibly the best story the game has ever told. Whether you play it now or later, Natlan is worth the conversation.
