Kingdom Come Deliverance 2 (Warhorse Studios, 2025). 2 vung dat: Trosky va Kuttenberg. 100+ gio noi dung. Combat cai tien voi sword fighting mo phong. Metacritic: 94.

Phan dau cua Kingdom Come Deliverance (2018) la tua game kho chieu nhat lich su RPG: khong co phep thuat, khong co nhan vat anh hung, chi co Henry — mot anh tho ren vung nong thon khong biet doc khong biet viet — trong mot Bohemia lich su thu 15 chinh xac den tu tung chi tiet. Va no kho den muc nhieu nguoi bo cuoc. Phan 2 giu nguyen ADN do nhung lam cho moi thu de tiep can hon, sau sac hon, va lon hon.

The Gioi: Hai Vung Dat, 100+ Gio Noi Dung

KCD2 bat dau ngay sau khi phan 1 ket thuc va chia lam hai map rieng biet. Trosky la vung nong thon quen thuoc cua KCD1 nhung mo rong dang ke. Kuttenberg — thanh pho that su tu lich su Bohemia — la thanh pho lon nhat trong mot RPG open world ke tu Novigrad cua The Witcher 3. O day co ca khu tan ngu, cho dem, guilds va mot nen kinh te thank pho song dong cho phep Henry leo len hay tut xuong theo hieu biet cua minh ve chinh tri va xa hoi.

Combat: Sword Fighting Mo Phong That Su

He thong kiem thuat cua KCD1 da noi tieng vi phuc tap — bat buoc nguoi choi hoc thuc su thay vi chi spam attack. KCD2 giu nguyen triet ly do nhung cai tien dang ke: feedback tot hon khi blocking, che do tap luyen mo rong giup nguoi moi bat kip, va dueling system moi cho phep Henry tu do hon trong phong cach chien dau. Lan dau tien trong KCD2 ban ha duoc mot kiem si giau kinh nghiem theo dung quy tac la mot trai nghiem khong the mo ta bang loi.

Henry: Tu Anh Tho Ren Den Nhan Vat Phuc Tap

Nhung gi Warhorse Studios thuc hien voi Henry trong KCD2 la mot trong nhung chuyen tiep nhan vat tot nhat trong lich su RPG. Henry trong phan 1 la nguoi ngoai — bat luc, khong biet gi. Henry trong phan 2 van phai hoc hoi, nhung bay gio anh ta co agency thuc su va dua ra nhung quyet dinh co hau qua that su. Cac dialogue choices phan anh lich su cua Henry va anh nhin doi voi the gioi — khong co cai gi goi la "good choice" hay "evil choice" don gian.

Authenticity: Czech Team Viet Ve Lich Su Cua Chinh Ho

Warhorse Studios la studio Czech — va ho viet ve Bohemia thu 15 voi su hieu biet va tinh yeu khong the gia. Moi kien truc, phong tuc, am nhac va doi thoai duoc nghien cuu tu tai lieu lich su that. KCD2 khong chi la mot RPG hay — no la mot tac pham van hoa quan trong cua Czech Republic, duoc the gioi gaming cong nhan.

Danh Gia: 9.4/10 — RPG Kieu Mau Cua Thap Nien 2020s

Kingdom Come Deliverance 2 la chung minh rang mot RPG khong can phep thuat hay do hoa phan quang de tao ra su dac sac. No dat ra tieu chuan moi cho authenticity trong storytelling va depth trong he thong gameplay. 9.4/10, va dat ten vao danh sach RPG tiet mo moi nguoi can choi truoc khi chet cung cac bieu tuong nhu Planescape Torment hay Baldur's Gate 2.
