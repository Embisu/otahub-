GTA 6: Everything We Know About the Biggest Game of 2026

Vice City tro lai, Ian vat nu chinh dau tien trong lich su GTA, va mot the gioi mo rong hua hen thay doi dinh nghia open-world.

Vice City returns, GTA's first female lead, and an open world promising to redefine the genre.

Summary
GTA 6 ra mat mua thu 2026 tren PS5 va Xbox Series X/S. Nhan vat chinh la Lucia, co the choi ca Jason. Boi canh: Leonida (Vice City hien dai). Gia: 80 USD / 2 trieu VND.
GTA 6 launches fall 2026 on PS5 and Xbox Series X/S. Main protagonist is Lucia, with Jason playable too. Setting: Leonida (modern Vice City). Price: $80 / 2 million VND.

Muoi hai nam sau GTA V va hon mot the ky tuong tuong tu nhung leaks khong ngung, Rockstar Games chinh thuc dat lich: Grand Theft Auto VI se dap xuong cac ke game vao mua thu 2026. Khong phai PC nguyen ban, khong phai remaster, ma la mot tua game xay dung tu dau voi ngan sach uoc tinh tren 2 ty USD.

Boi Canh: Leonida va Vice City Moi

Leonida la ten bang gia dinh tac gia dat cho tieu bang gia dinh Florida trong the gioi GTA. Thanh pho trung tam van la Vice City, nhung lan nay no khong chi la mot thanh pho neon lon tieng bang gio ma la mot do thi song dong voi vung ngoai o, dam lay, rung nhiet doi Everglades va ca cac thanh pho nho ven bien. Theo thong tin reo ra truoc khi ra mat, ban do cua GTA 6 se lon hon GTA V va Red Dead Redemption 2 cong lai, voi chu ky ngay dem, thoi tiet dong va he sinh thai song dong hon bao gio het.

Lucia: Nhan Vat Nu Chinh Dau Tien Trong Lich Su GTA

Day la dieu khien GTA 6 tro nen lich su theo mot nghia khac. Lucia, mot phu nu tre Latin America mang an tich toi pham, la nhan vat chinh ma nguoi choi se song cung trong phan lon trai nghiem. Cung voi co la Jason, ban trai cua co, hai nguoi tao nen mot cap doi rui ro kieu Bonnie va Clyde ma Rockstar xay dung xu huong co tinh den hanh trinh truong thanh, khong chi la hanh dong thuan tuy. Trailer 2 cho thay Lucia trong nhieu canh hanh dong: truoc chop tien, chuyen phi phap xuyen quoc gia va ca nhung khoanh khac ben le sau nhung vu cuop day mau.

The Gioi Song Dong: NPC Co Tri Nho, Kinh Te Thay Doi

Rockstar da im lang nhung ky su va nha phat trien trong nhieu nam, nhung nhung gi lo ra tu nguon noi bo tiet lo rang GTA 6 se co he NPC tien tien nhat tung duoc xay dung trong mot open-world game. Cac NPC se nho mat ban sau nhieu ngay choi, co the khieu kien neu ban thiet hai tai san cua ho, va phan ung khac nhau tuy thoi diem trong ngay. He kinh te cung thay doi theo hanh dong cua nguoi choi theo quy mo lon hon nhieu so voi GTA V: gia bat dong san tang khi ban lam khu vuc tro nen noi tieng vi toi pham, tin tuc trong game phan anh vu cuop ban vua thuc hien.

Multiplayer: GTA Online 2.0

GTA Online van tao ra hang trieu USD moi thang cho Rockstar du GTA V da ra mat nam 2013, nen viec cong ty khong nho toan bo len GTA 6 Online la dieu hoan toan co the hieu. Theo xac nhan chinh thuc, GTA 6 Online se ra mat sau game don choi khoang vai thang, voi quy mo server lon hon, cac event theo mua va mot nen kinh te online tinh vi hon. Rockstar cam ket khong co pay-to-win, chi co cosmetics va noi dung narrative.

Gia Va Phien Ban Ra Mat

GTA 6 se co gia 80 USD (khoang 2 trieu VND) tren PS5 va Xbox Series X/S, khong co phien ban PC ra mat cung luc, theo Rockstar. Mot phien ban Collector's Edition tri gia 150 USD bao gom art book, pin, mo hinh nhan vat va noi dung in-game da duoc tiet lo. Viet Nam khong co nha phan phoi chinh thuc, nguoi choi co the mua qua PSN Store chau A hoac Xbox Store voi gia dola giu nguyen.

Khi chung toi lan dau tien cho Lucia di bo trong Vice City, chung toi da biet day se la tua game dinh nghia lai toan bo the loai. Aaron Garbut, Art Director, Rockstar Games

Danh Gia Tong Quan

GTA 6 mang tren minh ap luc khong lo: no la sequel cua tua game ban chay nhat moi thoi dai, xay dung sau mot thap ky va voi ngan sach dang kinh nac. Nhung tung manh thong tin lo ra deu chi ra rang Rockstar dang dua vao day moi thu da hoc duoc tu Red Dead Redemption 2 va GTA Online de tao ra mot the gioi khong chi lon ve mat dia ly ma con sau sac hon ve mat narrative va song dong hon ve mat he sinh thai. Mua thu 2026 se la mot dip dang nho.

Twelve years after GTA V and over a decade of relentless anticipation, Rockstar Games has finally set the date: Grand Theft Auto VI arrives fall 2026. Not a PC port, not a remaster, but a ground-up build with an estimated budget north of two billion dollars.

Setting: Leonida and a New Vice City

Leonida is what Rockstar calls their fictional Florida stand-in. Vice City is back as the hub, but it is no longer just a glittering neon skyline. The map reportedly covers Vice City proper, outer suburbs, Everglades-style wetlands, tropical forests and smaller coastal towns. Leaks suggest the total playable area exceeds GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2 combined, with a full day-night cycle, dynamic weather and a living ecosystem.

Lucia: GTA's First Female Protagonist

This is what makes GTA 6 historically significant beyond budget. Lucia, a young Latin American woman with a criminal record, is the primary playable character. Alongside her is Jason, her boyfriend, creating a Bonnie-and-Clyde duo that Rockstar frames around growth and consequence, not just action. Trailer 2 showed Lucia across a bank heist, a cross-border smuggling run, and quieter character moments between the chaos.

A Living World: NPCs with Memory, a Dynamic Economy

Leaked internal documents and developer hints point to an NPC system more advanced than anything in an open-world game. NPCs will remember your face across multiple in-game days, can file civil complaints if you damage their property, and react differently depending on time of day. The economy shifts with player behavior at scale: property values change in neighborhoods you terrorize, in-game news broadcasts reference your recent heist.

Multiplayer: GTA Online 2.0

GTA Online still generates millions per month for Rockstar despite GTA V launching in 2013, so it is no surprise the studio is not migrating everything to GTA 6 Online at once. Confirmed for a staggered launch a few months after single-player, GTA 6 Online features larger servers, seasonal events and a more sophisticated online economy. Rockstar has committed to no pay-to-win, cosmetics and narrative content only.

Pricing and Launch Editions

GTA 6 is priced at $80 USD on PS5 and Xbox Series X/S, with no simultaneous PC release per Rockstar. A Collector's Edition at $150 includes an art book, pin set, figurine and in-game content. Vietnamese players can purchase through the Asian PSN Store or Xbox Store at the listed dollar price.

When we first walked Lucia through Vice City, we knew this would be the game that redefines the whole genre. Aaron Garbut, Art Director, Rockstar Games

Overall Assessment

GTA 6 carries enormous pressure: it is the sequel to the best-selling game of all time, built over a decade with a staggering budget. Every piece of information that has leaked suggests Rockstar is channeling everything learned from Red Dead Redemption 2 and GTA Online into a world that is not just geographically large but narratively deeper and ecologically more alive than anything before it. Fall 2026 will be a landmark moment.
