Berserk Final Arc: Studio Gaga Confirms the End Is Approaching

Studio Gaga tiep buoc Kentaro Miura voi su ton trong tuyet doi — Berserk arc cuoi dang duoc thuc hien voi chat luong xung dang voi di san cua manga huyen thoai.

Nearly five years after continuing Miura's work, the Berserk continuation team speaks clearly for the first time: the path to the ending is clear.

Summary
Berserk arc cuoi: Casca phuc hoi tri nho, Fantasia Arc, dao Skellig, Elfhelm. Studio Gaga (hoc tro cua Miura) tiep tuc dua tren ghi chu cua ong. Manga van dang phat hanh 2025.
Studio Gaga confirms Berserk is in its final arc. Continuation team: Kouji Mori + Studio Gaga. Miura left detailed notes through to the end. Published in Young Animal, no confirmed end date.

Khi Kentaro Miura qua doi vao thang 5/2021 sau 33 nam gan bo voi Berserk, the gioi manga chung kien mot mat mat khong the bu dap. Nhung Miura da de lai du ghi chu va chi tiet cot truyen de nhung nguoi hoc tro cua ong tai Studio Gaga co the tiep tuc — va nhung gi ho thuc hien dang chung to day la cach ton vinh di san dung dan nhat.

Casca Phuc Hoi — Khoanh Khac 30 Nam Cho Doi

Sau Eclipse, Casca mat di tri nho va tro thanh mot dua tre trong co the nguoi lon — suot gan 30 nam xuat ban manga, doc gia cho doi khoanh khac co phuc hoi. Khi dieu do cuoi cung xay ra tai dao Elfhelm, phan ung cua cong dong la mot trong nhung khoanh khac cam xuc nhat trong lich su manga. Nhung su phuc hoi do khong co nghia la hanh phuc — nhung ky uc cua Casca bao gom ca Eclipse, va co phai doi mat voi cau hoi: lieu co co the tha thu cho Guts khong?

Fantasia Arc — The Gioi Berserk Thay Doi Mai Mai

Fantasia Arc danh dau su chuyen doi lon nhat trong the gioi Berserk: ranh gioi giua the gioi nguoi va the gioi linh hon bi xoa bo — monsters, spirits va cac thuc the huyen bi tu do xuat hien trong thuc tai. Griffith, voi danh hieu Femto va than chien tranh moi cua the gioi, da bien dieu nay thanh hien thuc nhu mot phan ke hoach thong tri. Day khong con chi la cau chuyen cua Guts nua — day la cau chuyen ve so phan cua ca nhan loai.

Studio Gaga Va Cam Ket Voi Di San Miura

Dieu dang chu y nhat ve nhung chuong Berserk sau khi Miura mat la chung khong cam thay nhu mot san pham thay the. Studio Gaga — nhung nguoi da lam viec ben canh Miura nhieu nam — hieu ro ngon ngu hinh anh, nhip dieu tuong thuat va triet hoc dằng sau tung trang. Ho su dung ghi chu va rough sketches cua Miura nhu la ban, va ket qua la nhung chuong co the dung ngang voi phan noi dung goc.

Ket Thuc Se Nhu The Nao?

Dua tren nhung gi Miura tung chia se trong cac cuoc phong van, ong muon Berserk ket thuc voi "anh sang" chu khong phai bong toi hoan toan. Dieu nay goi y rằng Guts se co co hoi doi mat voi Griffith trong mot tran chien cuoi cung — va ket cuc, du the nao, se khong don gian la mot ben thang ben thua. Berserk chua bao gio don gian nhu vay.

In a rare interview with Young Animal magazine, Studio Gaga and Kouji Mori spoke clearly for the first time about where Berserk is heading: toward an ending. Not the uncertain conclusion fans feared when Kentaro Miura passed in 2021, but an intentional, structured conclusion that Miura himself prepared carefully before his death.

Miura's Legacy: The Notes He Left Behind

What gives the continuation team their confidence is the archive Miura left. According to Kouji Mori, Miura was meticulous about story structure and documented his plans in multiple forms: plot sketches, draft dialogues and even notes on the shading techniques required for the final pages. Mori, Miura's childhood friend, confirms that Miura told him the full ending of Berserk multiple times, to the point where Mori can reconstruct it as faithfully as possible with Studio Gaga's support.

Current Arc: Elfhelm and What's Coming

Berserk is currently in the Elfhelm arc, where Guts and his companions have reached the island of the Elves. This is where Miura intended to build toward a confrontation between Griffith and Guts with the highest personal depth, across the backdrop of a profound friendship destroyed by betrayal. The continuation team assures readers that Elfhelm is not filler but where multiple storylines converge before the conclusion. Some details of "the last battle" are already clear in Miura's sketches and the team is producing accordingly.

The Continuation Philosophy: Respecting the Source

Studio Gaga approaches continuing Berserk with a foundational principle: no speculation, only execute what Miura left. When facing situations Miura's notes do not clearly address, they choose the option least far from established patterns rather than expressing their own creativity. The line art is kept close to the original style, which is challenging given Miura was among the most technically skilled manga artists in history. Mori says that in some pages he can hear Miura "talking" through the old rough sketches.

When Does Berserk End?

This question still has no official answer. The continuation team refuses to set a specific deadline as they feel quality matters more than speed. However, based on the current release pace (roughly one chapter per month and a half) and the estimated remaining content per expert reader analysis, many predict Berserk could conclude somewhere between 2028 and 2030. Nothing is certain when every chapter is a renewed promise.

Miura told me the ending of Berserk many times. I understand it clearly. My mission is to bring it to readers the way Miura would have wanted. Kouji Mori, Berserk continuation lead
