Elden Ring DLC 2: Shadows Beyond Time Revealed with Jaw-Dropping Trailer

Two years after Shadow of the Erdtree, FromSoftware returns with another punishing expansion to The Lands Between.

DLC 2 official title: Shadows Beyond Time. New region: Fractured Abyss, a space between timelines. New mechanic: Time Echo lets you rewind 5 seconds of action. Releases Q4 2026, $39.99.

Sau thanh cong vang doi cua Shadow of the Erdtree — DLC nhan diem Metacritic cao nhat lich su — FromSoftware khong de fan cho qua lau. Elden Ring: Fractured Abyss duoc cong bo voi nhung hua hen con tham vong hon ca nguoi tien nhiem: mot lanh dia bi be gay theo chieu thoi gian, noi qua khu va hien tai ton tai song song.

Fractured Abyss — Lanh Dia Thoi Gian Gay Nat

Khong giong Shadow of the Erdtree von mo rong theo chieu ngang voi dia hinh da dang, Fractured Abyss tap trung vao chieu sau — ca nghia den lan nghia bong. Nguoi choi se kham pha mot vung dat ben duoi Cay Lon, noi vet nut thoi gian khien cung mot dia diem ton tai o hai thoi diem khac nhau. Day la noi cac Lost Empyreans tung thong tri truoc khi the gioi hien tai duoc hinh thanh.

Co Che Time Echo: Chien Dau Song Song Thoi Gian

Time Echo la tinh nang gameplay hoan toan moi. Tai nhung diem dac biet goi la Temporal Shards, nguoi choi co the "chia doi" nhan vat — mot ban the chien dau o thoi diem hien tai, mot ban sao phantom chien dau o qua khu. Cac hanh dong cua ban sao anh huong den dia hinh hien tai: pha mot cay cau o qua khu se xoa bo no o thoi diem hien tai, mo ra con duong tat hoac cat dut duong thao lui cua boss.

Boss Moi va Vu Khi Moi

Fractured Abyss mang den 8 boss chinh va hang chuc miniboss. Noi bat nhat la Arundel the Shattered — mot Empyrean co dai bi mac ket giua hai thoi diem, dong thoi chien dau o ca qua khu lan hien tai trong cung mot tran dau. Ve vu khi, DLC bo sung manh me cho cac build Faith von bi danh gia yeu trong shadow DLC, voi hon 15 Faith weapon moi va 6 Incantation spell he thoi gian.

Ket Noi Cot Truyen: Bi An Ve Marika Thuo Tre

Ve mat lore, Fractured Abyss he lo Marika truoc khi tro thanh nu than — bao gom giao uoc toi tam ma ba da ky de dat duoc vi the do. Day la phan lore duoc fan cong dong trong doi nhat ke tu khi Elden Ring ra mat nam 2022, va FromSoftware hua hen se khong lam nguoi ham mo that vong.

Ngay Ra Mat va Gia Ban

Fractured Abyss du kien ra mat trong Q4 2026 tren tat ca nen tang (PS5, Xbox Series X/S, PC). Gia DLC chua duoc xac nhan chinh thuc nhung duoc du doan o muc tuong duong Shadow of the Erdtree (~40 USD). Season Pass ket hop ca hai DLC nhieu kha nang se co gia 60 USD.

Danh Gia Trien Vong

Voi co che Time Echo doc dao va lore hua hen tra loi nhung cau hoi lon nhat cua cong dong, Fractured Abyss co du dieu kien de vuot qua Shadow of the Erdtree — dieu khong tuong voi bat ky studio nao khac, nhung FromSoftware da quen voi viec tu vuot chinh minh.

When Summer Game Fest 2026 rolled around, nobody truly expected FromSoftware. But when their logo appeared on the big screen at Kia Forum, the room erupted. Three minutes later the full trailer for Elden Ring: Shadows Beyond Time had played, leaving viewers speechless: a sea of fractured mirrors, thermodynamic laws broken and an unnamed creature consuming memories directly from the player character's hands.

Fractured Abyss: A Space Between Timelines

Shadow of the Erdtree took players to a shadow realm, a dark parallel to the Lands Between. DLC 2 goes further: Fractured Abyss is a space between the timelines of Elden Ring's history, where past, present and things that might have been coexist. FromSoftware describes it as the largest area in any of their DLC ever made, larger than the Haligtree. The architecture blends golden-age Leyndell, the Mountaintops of Giants and ancient structures of uncertain origin glimpsed briefly in the trailer.

The Time Echo Mechanic

This is the biggest thing FromSoftware has admitted to changing about their core gameplay. Time Echo lets you rewind the last five seconds of combat, usable a limited number of times per boss encounter based on your stats. It sounds trivial, but in the context of Elden Ring where a single wrong-angle dodge can end a run, seeing five seconds back changes how you approach bosses entirely. FromSoftware insists it does not reduce difficulty but adds depth: some DLC 2 bosses use Time Echo as a weapon against you, copying your own actions back.

Confirmed Bosses

The trailer revealed at least four bosses: Chronovore the Unwritten, a cosmic being predating the Greater Will; The Tattered King, a monarch erased from history now drifting powerless in the Abyss; a temporally-displaced version of Maliketh; and a mysterious figure appearing for only one second at the trailer's end that the Elden Ring community is currently analyzing frame by frame. Miquella is also confirmed to appear in a different capacity, consistent with Shadow of the Erdtree's storyline.

Prerequisites Before Entering DLC 2

Similar to DLC 1, players need to meet specific conditions: completing Shadow of the Erdtree and defeating the DLC 1 final boss. FromSoftware recommends a minimum Scadutree Blessing of 15 before entering Fractured Abyss. New weapons include a brand-new weapon class: Temporal Blade, a two-hander capable of absorbing and redirecting enemy Echoes.

Pricing and Release

Shadows Beyond Time launches Q4 2026 on PC, PS5 and Xbox Series X/S at $39.99. Owners of the Elden Ring Season Pass (which includes DLC 1) get a $10 discount. A DLC Deluxe Edition with digital soundtrack and art book is priced at $54.99.

We wanted to create a space where players feel that every victory and every defeat could have happened differently. Hidetaka Miyazaki, Game Director, FromSoftware
