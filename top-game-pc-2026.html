2026 la mot trong nhung nam game PC phong phu nhat trong thap ky — khong phai vi so luong, ma vi chat luong nhat quan. Khong co "nam kho han" khi moi quy deu co it nhat mot bom tan dang choi. Duoi day la 10 tua game PC hay nhat nam 2026, xep hang dua tren review tu cong dong va gia tri thuc te.

1. GTA 6 (Rockstar Games)

Khong co gi ban cai ve vi tri so 1 — GTA 6 la game duoc cho doi nhieu nhat trong lich su va no da xung dang voi su cho doi do. The gioi mo Leonida rong lon den muc GTA 5 trong nho be, dual protagonist story giua Jason va Lucia la tot nhat Rockstar tung viet, va muc do detail trong moi goc pho vuot xa bat ky game open world nao tu truoc den nay. 10/10.

2. Elden Ring: Fractured Abyss (FromSoftware)

DLC thu hai cua Elden Ring tiep tuc truyen thong cua Shadow of the Erdtree — lon hon nhieu nguoi nghi, kho hon nhieu nguoi muon, va thoa man hon bat ky dieu gi. Fractured Abyss dan nguoi choi vao mot vung dat ben duoi vung dat — lore cuoi cung ve nguon goc cua Greater Will duoc he lo. 9.5/10.

3. Death Stranding 2: On The Beach (Kojima Productions)

Hideo Kojima lai mot lan nua lam ra thu khong ai lam duoc: mot trai nghiem delivery simulator ky la, cam xuc, va thuc ra la ve ket noi con nguoi. DS2 mo rong ra khoi nuoc My va dua Sam den nhung vung dat moi voi nhung triet ly moi — va ban nhac nen cua Woodkid tiep tuc la dinh cao cua am nhac game. 9.3/10.

4. Monster Hunter Wilds: Titan Rift (Capcom)

DLC dau tien cua Monster Hunter Wilds mang den vung dat Titan Rift voi 8 quai vat hoan toan moi va 3 returning monster duoc redesign tu dau. Endgame loop duoc cai thien dang ke, va cong dong MH dang danh gia day la DLC MH hay nhat ke tu Iceborne. 9.1/10.

5-10: Nhung Gem Khong The Bo Qua

Vi tri 5 den 10 trong top game PC 2026 bao gom: Alan Wake 3 (Remedy tiep tuc meta-fiction horrror voi dinh cao moi — 9.0/10), Hollow Knight: Silksong (cuoi cung ra mat va xung dang voi moi su cho doi — 9.4/10), Hades II 1.0 full release (Supergiant tiep tuc cong thuc roguelike hoan hao — 9.5/10), Avowed (Obsidian cuoi cung tro lai dinh cao RPG — 8.8/10), Crimson Desert (bat ngo lon nhat nam — 8.5/10) va Stellar Blade PC (port hoan hao voi noi dung DLC — 8.8/10).

Ket Luan: 2026 La Nam Cua Game PC

Neu ban chi co the choi 3 game trong nam 2026, hay chon GTA 6, Elden Ring Fractured Abyss va Hollow Knight Silksong — ba tua game dai dien cho ba phong cach thiet ke khac nhau nhung deu dat dinh cao tuyet doi cua the loai. 2026 se duoc nho den nhu mot trong nhung nam game vi dai nhat trong lich su PC gaming.

2026 is a landmark year for PC gaming: the mega-blockbuster GTA 6 eventually reaches PC (albeit a bit late), but Elden Ring DLC 2 and several excellent indie gems also compete for your attention. OtaHub compiles the 10 games worthy of being the year's best on PC, covering both blockbusters and independents, both already available and recently launched.

1. Elden Ring: Shadows Beyond Time (DLC 2)

With PC being the primary platform for the majority of Elden Ring players, having FromSoftware's DLC 2 appear anywhere other than number one is unthinkable. Fractured Abyss, the Time Echo mechanic and new bosses are what finally arrived after Shadow of the Erdtree. If you have not played Elden Ring yet, 2026 is the year to start from the base game with all DLC already available.

2. Civilization VII

Firaxis finally announced and released Civilization VII after years of fan anticipation. This time there are fundamental changes: an Age system changes the rules of play across different historical eras, and players must adapt their strategy rather than developing one civilization from beginning to end. Some veterans are unhappy with the magnitude of change, but many consider this the most valuable advancement since Civ V.

3. Age of Darkness: Final Stand

Age of Darkness: Final Stand is the indie discovery of the year. An RTD (real-time defense) game with extraordinary difficulty and a Night Wave mechanic creating terrifying castle defense nights unlike anything else. Made by PlaySide Studios on a non-blockbuster budget but overwhelmingly positive reviews sustained throughout. One of the biggest indie hits of 2026.

4. Monster Hunter Wilds

Capcom released Monster Hunter Wilds, the series' first truly open-world installment with a living natural ecosystem. Hunting remains the focus but now features dynamic weather, living ecosystems and monsters whose behavior changes with their environment. The PC version on Steam has better optimization than World, great news after that predecessor's troubled PC port.

5. Hollow Knight: Silksong

Finally. Hollow Knight: Silksong launched February 2026 after years of being "most anticipated game of every year." Team Cherry delivered on expectations: Hornet has gameplay distinct from the Knight, a large map and difficulty tuned to satisfy both newcomers and veterans. Metacritic 94.

6. Dragon's Dogma 3

Capcom continues with Dragon's Dogma 3 following DD2's success. This installment keeps the unique Pawn system while expanding the world, improving AI and adding new classes. Combat remains the primary reason to play, but narrative is clearly more invested this time based on DD2 feedback.

7. Satisfactory 2 (Early Access)

Satisfactory 2 returned to Early Access mid-2026, bringing features fans requested since the first version: more complex electricity systems, passenger transport, cargo elevators and terraforming. Coffee Stain Studios continues adding content weekly in an agile approach. The year's favorite factory game.

8. Path of Exile 2 (1.0 Full Release)

Grinding Gear Games officially launched Path of Exile 2 version 1.0 following Early Access. Combat feels entirely new compared to PoE1, safer and more visually polished while retaining the deep build theory of the predecessor. Endgame uses a new Atlas system with Breach, Delirium and richly varied Mapping.

9. S.T.A.L.K.E.R. 2: Heart of Chornobyl (DLC)

S.T.A.L.K.E.R. 2 launched its base game in late 2025 with some technical rough edges, but 2026's Game of the Year Edition and "Dead City" DLC addressed most of those issues. GSC Game World hit their stride after the base game launched, and Dead City is the best content they have made, featuring a radiated Kyiv district and a new ghost hunter mechanic.

10. Hades II (Full Release)

Supergiant Games officially closed Early Access on Hades II mid-2026. Melinoe is among the studio's best-built protagonists, and the roguelike system is upgraded with several new mechanics. If Hades 1 set the benchmark, Hades 2 raises that benchmark just a little higher.
