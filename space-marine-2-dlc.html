Warhammer 40K Space Marine 2: Chaos Rising DLC and Season 2 Content

Sau hon mot nam, Space Marine 2 van la tua game ban chay va Chaos Rising hua se lam hai long ca fan campaign lan PvP.

Over a year later, Space Marine 2 remains a bestseller and Chaos Rising promises to satisfy both campaign and PvP fans.

Chaos Rising DLC launches August 15, 2026, priced $24.99. Adds 2 campaign chapters with Titus facing Chaos Space Marines. Operations mode gets new faction: Chaos Marine with 3 distinct classes. 3 new PvP maps.

Khi Space Marine 2 ra mat nam 2024, no ban duoc hon 5 trieu ban trong vong 2 tuan, con so ma ngay ca Focus Entertainment cung phai ngac nhien. Mot nam sau, game van duy tri nguoi choi tich cuc nho cap nhat Operations miễn phi thuong xuyen. Va bay gio Saber Interactive chinh thuc cong bo DLC lon dau tien: Chaos Rising, du hua se lam day nhung khoang trong ma campaign goc de lai.

Campaign Moi: Titus Gap Chaos

Hai chapter campaign moi cua Chaos Rising tiep noi truc tiep sau ket thuc cua campaign goc. Titus va Dreadnought squad duoc cai lich den mot he thong hanh tinh bi cat lien lac, noi ma mot faction Chaos Space Marine tu Noise Marines cua Emperor's Children dang thuc hien mot nghi le bi an. Chapter 1 dien ra tren Daemon World, mot hanh tinh bi bien doi boi Warp energy, voi level design co nhieu vertical traversal hon game goc. Chapter 2 la tran cuoi co boss fight duoc Saber Interactive mieu ta la "lon nhat chung toi tung thiet ke theo nghia den".

Operations: Choi Voi Chaos Space Marine

Day la dieu fan Operations mode doi lau nhat. Chaos Rising cho phep nguoi choi choi voi faction Chaos Space Marine trong Operations, voi 3 class moi: Heretic Astartes (melee fighter dung danh vung chac), Sorcerer (caster ho tro doi bang Warp spell) va Obliterator (heavy ranged voi kha nang bien doi vu khi). Moi class Chaos co cay ky nang rieng biet khac hoan toan voi 6 class cua Space Marine goc, khong chi la re-skin. Leaderboard Operations se tach rieng giua Space Marine va Chaos Marine.

PvP: 3 Map Moi Va Mode Moi

Eternal War (PvP) nhan duoc 3 map moi trong Chaos Rising: Daemon Forge (nhung xuong duc vu khi bi Chaos chiem), Void Chapel (mot nha thu troi day trong khong gian bi da than Chaos xam nhap) va Hive District Theta (khu do thi tang cao bi phong toa). Ngoai ra, mode moi Ritual Sabotage yeu cau mot doi bao ve vong nghi le trong khi doi kia pha huy: mot mode asymmetric dau tien cua game co tu luong kha lon.

Noi Dung Mien Phi Kem Theo

Kem theo DLC tra phi, Saber Interactive cung phat hanh Season 2 Update mien phi cho tat ca nguoi choi: them 2 Operation moi (co the choi voi hoac khong DLC), cap nhat Armoury them 50 cosmetic moi theo phong cach Chaos (mua rieng hoac earn qua Operation), va cai thien matchmaking cho tat ca modes. Nguoi co game goc khong can mua Chaos Rising de troi duoc 2 Operation mien phi.

Chaos Rising la thu chung toi muon lam ngay tu dau nhung can du thoi gian de lam dung. Chaos khong chi la Space Marine xau. Ho co triet ly va co ly do ton tai. Tim Willits, Studio Director, Saber Interactive

Lich Ra Mat Va Gia

Space Marine 2: Chaos Rising ra mat 15/8/2026 tren PC, PS5 va Xbox Series X/S, gia 24.99 USD. Nguoi co Gold Edition hoac Season Pass se nhan DLC khong can mua them. Pre-order DLC duoc tang them mot Chaos Terminator cosmetic set cho tat ca 3 class moi.

When Space Marine 2 launched in 2024, it sold over 5 million copies within two weeks, a number that even surprised Focus Entertainment. A year later, the game maintains active players through regular free Operations updates. And now Saber Interactive officially announces its first major DLC: Chaos Rising, promising to fill the gaps the original campaign left open.

New Campaign: Titus Meets Chaos

The two new Chaos Rising campaign chapters continue directly after the original campaign's conclusion. Titus and his Dreadnought squad are dispatched to a star system that has gone silent, where a Chaos Space Marine faction from the Noise Marines of Emperor's Children is conducting a mysterious ritual. Chapter 1 takes place on a Daemon World, a planet transformed by Warp energy, with level design featuring far more vertical traversal than the base game. Chapter 2 is the finale with a boss fight Saber Interactive describes as "the largest we have ever designed in a literal sense."

Operations: Play as Chaos Space Marine

This is what Operations mode fans have wanted longest. Chaos Rising allows players to play as the Chaos Space Marine faction in Operations, with 3 new classes: Heretic Astartes (solid melee fighter), Sorcerer (team support caster with Warp spells) and Obliterator (heavy ranged with weapon-transformation ability). Each Chaos class has an entirely distinct skill tree from the 6 original Space Marine classes, not merely reskins. Operations leaderboards will be separated between Space Marine and Chaos Marine.

PvP: 3 New Maps and a New Mode

Eternal War (PvP) receives 3 new maps in Chaos Rising: Daemon Forge (weapon foundries captured by Chaos), Void Chapel (a cathedral floating in space invaded by Chaos daemons) and Hive District Theta (a blockaded high-rise urban district). Additionally, new mode Ritual Sabotage requires one team to protect a ritual circle while the other team destroys it: the game's first asymmetric mode with a fairly high skill ceiling.

Free Content Included

Alongside the paid DLC, Saber Interactive releases a free Season 2 Update for all players: 2 new Operations (playable with or without the DLC), an Armoury update adding 50 new Chaos-themed cosmetics (purchasable separately or earnable through Operations), and matchmaking improvements across all modes. Base game owners do not need to buy Chaos Rising to access the 2 free Operations.

Chaos Rising is what we wanted to make from the beginning but needed the time to do properly. Chaos is not just evil Space Marines. They have philosophy and reason to exist. Tim Willits, Studio Director, Saber Interactive

Release Date and Pricing

Space Marine 2: Chaos Rising launches August 15, 2026 on PC, PS5 and Xbox Series X/S, priced at $24.99. Gold Edition or Season Pass owners receive the DLC at no additional cost. DLC pre-orders include a bonus Chaos Terminator cosmetic set for all 3 new classes.
