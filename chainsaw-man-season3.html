Aging Devil Arc: Tai Sao No Quan Trong

Trong cac arc cua Chainsaw Man phan 2, Aging Devil arc duoc nhieu fan manga danh gia la arc tan the la nhat. Arc xoay quanh mot thuc the co kha nang khien nguoi ta gia di ngay truoc mat, mot lo sot ve an tuong voi Tatsuki Fujimoto. Khong chi la cai dep bao luc, arc nay chieu sau tinh toan: cac nhan vat phai dau voi gia tri cua thoi gian, ky uc va lo hai bi qua lang. No cung la noi Asa Mitaka, nhan vat tu mua truoc, co arc ca nhan sau sac nhat cua co.

Doi Ngu San Xuat va Su Thay Doi

MAPPA giu lai Tatsu Miura lam director, nguoi da lam nen dau an voi chuyen dong camera cua cac canh chien dau o Season 2. Tuy nhien, co mot thay doi lon: Kiyotaka Oshiyama, nguoi da lam Character Designer cho Nier: Automata the Animation, gia nhap nhu Lead Animation Director. Theo thong bao, MAPPA da tang ngan sach san xuat len khoang 40% so voi Season 2, dieu nay the hien ro qua chat luong key frames trong teaser. CGI van duoc su dung cho cac canh hanh dong quy mo lon nhung se ket hop muen mang hon voi 2D.

Nhan Vat Moi Va Lich Phat Song

Season 3 gioi thieu it nhat ba nhan vat moi dang ke: Haruka Iseumi, mot trai tre ki la voi moi lien ket kho hieu voi Primal Devils; nguoi thay bong toi danh tieng va mot nhan vat nen cua Aging Devil ma Fujimoto giu kin den phut chot trong manga. Lich phat song la 13 tap trong mot mua, khong co split-cour theo tuyen bo cua MAPPA, bao dam tron ven arc trong mot luc.

Fujimoto Tham Gia Giam Sat

Mot tiet lo thu vi: Tatsuki Fujimoto, tac gia manga, lan nay tham gia truc tiep hon vao giam sat san xuat anime. Ong da xem truoc cac key frames, dua ra feedback va xac nhan rang mot so canh se duoc mo rong them thoi luong so voi manga goc, dac biet la canh mo dau cua arc. Day la du hieu tot cho nhung ai lo lang rang anime se cat gian noi dung quan trong.

Xem O Dau?

Chainsaw Man Season 3 phat song tren Crunchyroll voi sub tieng Anh. Viet Nam nam trong vung phat song cua Crunchyroll Dong Nam A. Ngoai ra MAPPA cung hop tac voi Netflix cho mot so thi truong nhat dinh, tuy nhien chi tiet chinh xac ve phan phoi chua duoc cong bo day du.

Aging Devil arc la arc toi muon lam dung nhat. No can su that tran cua cam xuc chu khong chi la su that tran cua hanh dong. Tatsu Miura, Director, MAPPA

Aniplex confirmed everything at Anime Expo Los Angeles in early June: Chainsaw Man Season 3 premieres October 2026 on Crunchyroll and other platforms. Alongside the announcement came a teaser trailer that silenced doubters, showcasing MAPPA's animation style taken to another level with improved lighting, movement and the kinetic feel of combat sequences.

The Aging Devil Arc: Why It Matters

Among the arcs of Chainsaw Man Part 2, the Aging Devil arc is widely considered by manga readers to be the most existentially disturbing. It centers on an entity capable of aging people before your eyes, a deeply clever fear metaphor by Tatsuki Fujimoto. Beyond the graphic violence, the arc goes philosophical: characters confront the value of time, memory and fear of being forgotten. It is also where Asa Mitaka, introduced in the previous season, receives her most substantial personal arc.

Production Team and Changes

MAPPA retained Tatsu Miura as director, the person responsible for the distinctive camera movement in Season 2 fight sequences. However there is one major change: Kiyotaka Oshiyama, who served as Character Designer on Nier: Automata the Animation, joins as Lead Animation Director. MAPPA reportedly increased the production budget by roughly 40% compared to Season 2, visible clearly in the teaser's key frame quality. CGI is still used for large-scale action sequences but will blend more seamlessly with 2D animation.

New Characters and Schedule

Season 3 introduces at least three significant new characters: Haruka Iseumi, a mysterious young man with an unclear connection to the Primal Devils; a notorious shadow instructor; and a background figure of the Aging Devil that Fujimoto kept hidden until the very end of the manga. The season runs 13 episodes with no split-cour format per MAPPA's statement, delivering the full arc in one continuous run.

Fujimoto's Direct Involvement

One interesting revelation: Tatsuki Fujimoto, the manga's author, is more directly involved in overseeing this season's production. He has reviewed key frames, provided feedback and confirmed that certain scenes will receive more runtime than the source manga, particularly the arc's opening. This is a good sign for those worried the anime might cut important story content.

Where to Watch

Chainsaw Man Season 3 streams on Crunchyroll with English subtitles. Vietnam is within Crunchyroll's Southeast Asia broadcast territory. MAPPA is also partnering with Netflix for certain markets, though full distribution details have not been fully announced.

The Aging Devil arc is the one I most wanted to get right. It requires an emotional rawness, not just an action rawness. Tatsu Miura, Director, MAPPA
