OtaHub Score: 9.0/10. 7.9-inch 1080p OLED screen. New Joy-Con with mouse mode. GameChat innovative. Dock upgraded to 4K 120fps. Strong launch titles: Mario Kart World, Donkey Kong Bananza. Price: $449.

Ba thang sau khi Nintendo Switch 2 ra mat, OtaHub co the noi voi su tu tin: day la console tot nhat Nintendo tung san xuat. Khong phai vi no la buoc nhay vot cong nghe lon nhat, ma vi Nintendo cuoi cung giai quyet nhung diem yeu kinh nien cua Switch 1 trong khi giu nguyen nhung diem manh da lam nen ten tuoi cua no. 449 USD la muc gia khong re, nhung voi nhung gi ban nhan lai, no xung dang.

Man Hinh 1080p OLED: Khac Biet Ro Rang

Man hinh 7.9 inch OLED 1080p cua Switch 2 la su nang cap ro rang nhat so voi Switch OLED 720p cu. Khong chi la do phan giai cao hon: mau sac sat hoa hon, do sang tot hon trong anh sang manh va thoi gian phan hoi nhanh hon. Khi choi Mario Kart World handheld, su khac biet so voi Switch 1 la qua ro de co the phu nhan. Kho khan duy nhat: man hinh lon hon lam may nang them mot chut va giam thoi luong pin xuong khoang 4-5 tieng choi thay vi 7-9 tieng cua Switch OLED.

Joy-Con 2: Cuoi Cung Khong Con Drift

Day la dieu fan da doi tu nhiều nam. Joy-Con 2 su dung co che hall effect sensor thay vi joystick co giam sat cu, loai bo hoan toan van de drift kinh nien cua dong game truoc. Ngoai ra, Joy-Con 2 co them mouse mode: khi dat may xuong ban, Joy-Con co the dung nhu chuot may tinh thong thuong. Tinh nang nay duoc tich hop trong Drag x Drive (game bong ro), Metroid Prime 4 va nhieu tua khac. Nhan cung lay va lat vao khop mua sac chat hon nhieu, loai bo cam giac lung lay cua Joy-Con cu.

GameChat: Doc Dao Nhung Can Thoi Gian

GameChat la tinh nang nhat ma Nintendo xay dung: chat thoai va video qua cam bien cua man hinh, co the share nhanh screenshot/video va tao nhom choi lien ket truc tiep qua game. Trong thuc te, GameChat hoat dong tot khi internet on dinh va rat tien loi cho nhung ai chi dung Switch 2 de choi voi ban be khong o cung thanh pho. Nhung so voi Discord hay cac ung dung ngoai, no van thieu nhieu tinh nang. Nintendo cuoi cung co co so ha tang online on dinh, nhung GameChat la buoc dau tien chu chua phai dich.

Launch Titles: Manh Nhat Trong Lich Su Nintendo

Launch window cua Switch 2 la launch window manh nhat trong lich su Nintendo. Mario Kart World (khong phai Mario Kart 9) la tua game mo rong lon nhat series voi open world giua cac circuit, 24 racer cung luc va track moi theo thoi tiet. Donkey Kong Bananza la platformer 3D dau tien dung ten DK lam nhan vat chinh tu Donkey Kong 64. Ca hai deu la tua game dat chat luong cao ngay tu launch, khac voi Switch 1 khi phai doi Breath of the Wild "cuu" may.

Hieu Nang: 4K Khi Dock, 1080p Handheld

Chip tuy chinh cua Switch 2 (dua tren Nvidia Ampere) xuat 4K 60fps khi dock voi TV ho tro, hoac 120fps 1080p trong mot so tua game toi uu hoa. Handheld giu 1080p o khoang 60fps on dinh trong phan lon tua game. Nhiet do may khi choi intensive game o dock cao hon Switch 1 nhung van nam trong nguong an toan. RAM 12GB va 256GB storage built-in la nang cap lon ve mat thuc te.

Switch 2 khong phai la may choi game manh nhat. Nhung no la may choi game tot nhat de thuc su mang theo ben minh va choi moi luc moi noi. OtaHub Reviewer, sau 3 thang su dung hang ngay

Ket Luan: 9.0/10

Nintendo Switch 2 la console dang mua nhat nam 2026 neu ban chua co mot may choi game cam tay. No giai quyet drift, nang cap man hinh va dock len 4K, giu nguyen khai niem hybrid ma Switch 1 da lam nen ten. Launch library manh, GameChat co tiem nang. Gia 449 USD cao hon Switch 1 launch nhung hoan toan phu hop voi nhung gi ban nhan lai. Mua di.

Three months after the Nintendo Switch 2 launch, OtaHub can state with confidence: this is the best hardware Nintendo has ever made. Not because it is the biggest technological leap, but because Nintendo finally resolved the chronic weaknesses of the Switch 1 while preserving the strengths that made it legendary. $449 is not cheap, but what you receive in return is worth it.

1080p OLED Display: A Clear Difference

The 7.9-inch 1080p OLED display is the most immediately obvious upgrade over the old Switch OLED's 720p. It is not only about resolution: colors are more vivid, brightness performance in strong lighting is superior and response time is faster. Playing Mario Kart World in handheld mode, the difference from Switch 1 is too obvious to deny. The only difficulty: the larger screen makes the device slightly heavier and reduces battery life to roughly 4-5 hours of play versus the Switch OLED's 7-9.

Joy-Con 2: Finally No More Drift

This is what fans have requested for years. Joy-Con 2 uses hall effect sensors instead of the old potentiometer joysticks, completely eliminating the chronic drift issue of the previous generation. Additionally, Joy-Con 2 adds a mouse mode: when placed on a flat surface, Joy-Con can function as a regular computer mouse. This feature is integrated in Drag x Drive (basketball game), Metroid Prime 4 and several others. The click-and-lock attachment mechanism is also notably more solid, eliminating the wobbly feeling of the original Joy-Con.

GameChat: Innovative but Needs Time

GameChat is Nintendo's most ambitious feature attempt: voice and video chat via the screen's built-in sensor, quick screenshot/video sharing and creating play groups linked directly through the game. In practice, GameChat works well when internet is stable and is genuinely convenient for playing with friends in different cities. But compared to Discord or external apps, it still lacks many features. Nintendo finally has stable online infrastructure, but GameChat is a first step not a destination.

Launch Titles: Nintendo's Strongest Ever

Switch 2's launch window is Nintendo's strongest in their history. Mario Kart World (not Mario Kart 9) is the series' largest expansion with an open world between circuits, 24 simultaneous racers and weather-dynamic new tracks. Donkey Kong Bananza is the first 3D platformer featuring DK as the main character since Donkey Kong 64. Both launched at high quality, unlike Switch 1 which essentially needed Breath of the Wild to "save" the hardware.

Performance: 4K Docked, 1080p Handheld

Switch 2's custom chip (Nvidia Ampere-based) outputs 4K 60fps when docked to a supported TV, or 120fps 1080p in some optimized titles. Handheld maintains stable 1080p at around 60fps in most games. Device temperature during intensive docked play is higher than Switch 1 but remains within safe limits. 12GB RAM and 256GB built-in storage are significant practical upgrades.

Switch 2 is not the most powerful gaming device. But it is the best gaming device to actually carry everywhere and play whenever, wherever. OtaHub Reviewer, after 3 months of daily use

Verdict: 9.0/10

Nintendo Switch 2 is the most worth-buying gaming device of 2026 if you do not already own a handheld. It fixes drift, upgrades the display and docked output to 4K, and retains the hybrid concept that made Switch 1 iconic. Strong launch library, GameChat has potential. $449 is higher than Switch 1's launch price but fully appropriate for what you receive. Buy it.
