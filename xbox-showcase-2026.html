Xbox Games Showcase 2026: Every Game Announced

Gears of War E-Day confirmed Xbox exclusive, Persona 6 revealed, Fable launching early 2027. Here's the complete recap of Xbox's biggest showcase of the year.

📌 Tom tat nhanh
Xbox Games Showcase 2026 dien ra ngay 7/6/2026, ky niem 25 nam thuong hieu Xbox. Su kien gioi thieu hon 20 tua game moi, phan cung gioi han Xbox Series X25, va xac nhan Gears of War: E-Day cung Clockwork Revolution la doc quyen Xbox vinh vien.

Phan Cung: Xbox Series X25 Ky Niem 25 Nam

Microsoft bat ngo mo dau show bang viec cong bo Xbox Series X25 Limited Edition, phien ban dac biet ky niem 25 nam thuong hieu Xbox tu 2001 den 2026. May co thiet ke vo trong suot hoai co lay cam hung tu Xbox doi dau, ket hop voi tong mau xanh la "OG Green" dac trung ma fan lau nam se nhan ra ngay lap tuc.

Bo Xbox Wireless Controller X25 Special Edition cung duoc ban kem hoac rieng le voi cung ngon ngu thiet ke. Ca hai san pham ra mat thang 11/2026 voi so luong gioi han tai mot so thi truong duoc chon. Day la lan dau tien Xbox ra mat phien ban phan cung dac biet quy mo lon ke tu Xbox One X, va theo nhan dinh ban dau, suc hut hoai niem cua thiet ke trong suot se khien may chay hang rat nhanh.

Gears of War: E-Day

Day la tua game duoc cho doi nhat toan bo show. Gears of War: E-Day la phan tien truyen cua series huyen thoai, lay boi canh dung ngay E-Day, tuc ngay dau tien lu Locust xuat hien tren be mat hanh tinh Sera va tan sat loai nguoi. Marcus Fenix va Dom Santiago luc nay chua phai nhung chien binh day dan ma nguoi choi quen thuoc, ho moi chi la nhung tan binh doi mat voi moi de doa chua tung thay.

The Coalition phat trien game tren Unreal Engine 5 va nhung gi trailer cho thay vuot xa moi ky vong ve do chi tiet moi truong. Microsoft xac nhan day la doc quyen vinh vien tren Xbox Series X|S va PC, khong phai timed exclusive nhu nhieu tua truoc. Game ra mat ngay 6 thang 10, 2026 va co ngay tren Game Pass tu ngay dau tien.

Fable

Playground Games, studio noi tieng voi Forza Horizon, cuoi cung da cho thay gameplay thuc su cua Fable. Ban reboot nay khong co tai tao Fable 1 hay Fable 2 ma xay dung mot the gioi Albion hoan toan moi voi nu nhan vat chinh va he thong dao duc duoc thiet ke lai tu dau. Chat hai huoc dac trung cua series van con do: nhung NPC ky quac, loi thoai cham biem va the gioi phan ung theo tung lua chon cua nguoi choi.

Sau nhieu nam im lang den muc cong dong gan nhu mat niem tin, Fable trong thuc su da san sang. Game ra mat ngay 23 thang 2, 2027 tren Xbox Series X|S va PC, co mat tren Game Pass tu ngay dau.

Persona 6

Atlus da pha vo truyen thong bang cach chon Xbox Showcase de cong bo Persona 6. Day la lan dau tien mot game Persona chinh thuc duoc cong bo song song tren nhieu nen tang ngay tu dau, bao gom Xbox, PlayStation va PC, thay vi doc quyen PlayStation trong nhieu nam roi moi porting. Quyet dinh nay phan anh thuc te: Persona 3 Reload va Persona 5 Royal da dat doanh so cuc ky tot tren Xbox, thuyet phuc Atlus thay doi chien luoc phan phoi.

Trailer he lo boi canh moi voi chu de am nhac noi bat va phong cach thiet ke nhan vat sac net hon bao gio het. Thong tin chi tiet ve gameplay va cot truyen van chua duoc tiet lo, nhung su xuat hien tai Xbox Showcase da du de cong dong Persona suc soi. Game du kien ra mat trong nam 2027.

Valor Mortis, Spyro va Cac Tua Dang Chu Y Khac

Valor Mortis la cai ten gay bat ngo nhat trong show. Game action Soulslike goc nhin thu nhat voi boi canh thoi Napoleon, trong do dien vien nguoi Phap Vincent Cassel long tieng cho chinh Napoleon Bonaparte. Phong cach visual gothic ket hop voi loi choi yeu cau phan xa cao trong rat hua hen. Game ra mat 24 thang 9, 2026 va co ngay tren Game Pass.

Spyro: A Realm Beyond xac nhan su tro lai cua con rong tim huyen thoai sau nhieu nam vang bong. Trailer dien anh dai gan 3 phut voi chat luong hinh anh an tuong, the gioi rong lon va am nhac mang dung hoi tho cua Spyro goc. Du kien ra mat mua Xuan 2027. Minecraft Dungeons 2 do Mojang phat trien noi tiep thanh cong cua phan dau voi he thong loot va dungeon mo rong dang ke, ra mat 29 thang 9, 2026.

Cac tua khac dang theo doi: Clockwork Revolution tu inXile Entertainment la RPG Steampunk duoc xac nhan doc quyen Xbox vinh vien; Crazy Taxi: World Tour cua Sega hoi sinh franchise kinh dien voi scale lon hon nhieu so voi ban goc, du kien 2027; Vivarium la tua game life-sim phieu luu lay cam hung tu anime cel-shaded voi phong cach hoan toan khac biet trong catalog Xbox; va JOIN US, game sinh ton ve giao phai tap trung vao multiplayer, tren Game Pass tu thang 3/2027.

Nhan Dinh Tong The

Xbox Games Showcase 2026 la mot trong nhung su kien an tuong nhat cua Microsoft trong nhieu nam tro lai day. Viec xac nhan Gears of War: E-Day va Clockwork Revolution la doc quyen vinh vien, khong phai timed exclusive, la tin hieu ro rang rang Xbox dang nghiem tuc dau tu vao he sinh thai rieng thay vi don thuan la nen tang phan phoi game.

2026 la nam Xbox tro lai thuc su: hardware ky niem co suc hut hoai niem manh, exclusive first-party da san sang, va Game Pass tiep tuc mo rong voi nhung tua AAA ngay tu ngay dau. Day la buc tranh khac han so voi Xbox hai nam truoc. OtaHub Editorial

Tuy nhien, phan lon nhung tua game lon nhat van huong den nam 2027, co nghia la cuoi nam 2026 Xbox gan nhu dat toan bo cuoc vao Gears of War: E-Day. Neu The Coalition thuc su mang den mot tua game xung tam ky vong, day se la mua le dang nho nhat cua Xbox ke tu 2018. Rieng voi Persona 6, su xuat hien tai Xbox Showcase la dau hieu manh me cho thay ranh gioi doc quyen giua cac console dang ngay cang mo nhat, va nguoi choi tren moi nen tang deu huong loi tu dieu do.

📌 Quick Summary
Xbox Games Showcase 2026 took place June 7, 2026, celebrating Xbox's 25th anniversary. The show revealed 20+ new games, the limited-edition Xbox Series X25 hardware, and confirmed Gears of War: E-Day and Clockwork Revolution as permanent Xbox exclusives.

Hardware: Xbox Series X25 Anniversary Edition

Microsoft opened the show with a surprise hardware announcement: the Xbox Series X25 Limited Edition, a special console celebrating 25 years of the Xbox brand from 2001 to 2026. The console features a translucent shell design inspired by the original Xbox, paired with the iconic "OG Green" color that long-time fans will immediately recognize.

A matching Xbox Wireless Controller X25 Special Edition will also be sold separately. Both products launch in November 2026 as a limited run in select markets. This is the most ambitious special-edition hardware Xbox has released since the Xbox One X, and the nostalgia factor alone is likely to drive it out of stock fast.

Gears of War: E-Day

The most anticipated reveal of the show. Gears of War: E-Day is a prequel to the entire Gears series, set on the day of the Locust emergence when the creatures first broke through the surface of Sera and began slaughtering humanity. Marcus Fenix and Dom Santiago are here as young soldiers, not the hardened veterans players remember, facing a threat no one has ever encountered before.

Developed by The Coalition on Unreal Engine 5, the trailer showcased a level of environmental detail that surpasses anything seen in the series so far. Microsoft confirmed this is a permanent Xbox console exclusive for Xbox Series X|S and PC, not a timed exclusive. The game launches October 6, 2026 and will be available on Game Pass from day one.

Fable

Playground Games, the studio behind Forza Horizon, finally showed real gameplay of Fable. Rather than recreating Fable 1 or Fable 2, this reboot builds an entirely new Albion with a female protagonist and a redesigned morality system. The series' signature humor is very much intact: eccentric NPCs, sharp wit, and a world that reacts to every choice the player makes.

After years of silence that had fans questioning whether the project was still alive, Fable looks genuinely ready. The game launches February 23, 2027 on Xbox Series X|S and PC, available on Game Pass from day one.

Persona 6

Atlus broke with tradition by choosing the Xbox Showcase to announce Persona 6. This is the first time a mainline Persona game has been announced simultaneously across multiple platforms from day one, including Xbox, PlayStation, and PC, rather than spending years as a PlayStation exclusive before porting. The decision reflects the commercial reality: Persona 3 Reload and Persona 5 Royal both performed exceptionally well on Xbox, convincing Atlus to shift strategy.

The trailer revealed a new setting with a strong musical theme and sharper character designs than any previous entry in the series. Gameplay and story details remain under wraps, but the Xbox Showcase reveal was enough to send the Persona community into a frenzy. The game is targeting a 2027 release.

Valor Mortis, Spyro and Other Notable Reveals

Valor Mortis was the biggest surprise of the show. A dark first-person action Soulslike set during the Napoleonic era, with French actor Vincent Cassel voicing Napoleon Bonaparte himself. The gothic visual style combined with demanding skill-based combat looks extremely promising. The game launches September 24, 2026 and will be on Game Pass from day one.

Spyro: A Realm Beyond confirms the long-awaited return of the purple dragon after years of silence. The nearly three-minute cinematic trailer featured stunning visuals, an expansive world, and music that genuinely captures the spirit of the original Spyro games. Expected to launch Spring 2027. Minecraft Dungeons 2, developed by Mojang, expands the loot and dungeon systems of the original significantly and launches September 29, 2026.

Other titles worth watching: Clockwork Revolution from inXile Entertainment, a Steampunk RPG confirmed as a permanent Xbox exclusive; Crazy Taxi: World Tour from Sega, reviving the beloved franchise at a much larger scale, targeting 2027; Vivarium, a cel-shaded life-sim adventure inspired by classic anime; and JOIN US, a multiplayer cult survival game coming to Game Pass in March 2027.

Final Take

Xbox Games Showcase 2026 stands out as one of Microsoft's most impressive shows in years. Confirming Gears of War: E-Day and Clockwork Revolution as permanent exclusives, not timed, signals a genuine commitment to building Xbox's own ecosystem rather than simply being a distribution platform.

2026 is Xbox's real comeback: anniversary hardware with strong nostalgic pull, first-party exclusives ready to deliver, and Game Pass continuing to grow with day-one AAA titles. This is a fundamentally different picture from Xbox two years ago. OtaHub Editorial

That said, with Fable and most of the marquee titles targeting 2027, the 2026 holiday season rests almost entirely on Gears of War: E-Day. If The Coalition delivers a game worthy of the expectations, this will be Xbox's most memorable holiday since 2018. As for Persona 6, its Xbox Showcase appearance is the clearest sign yet that console exclusivity boundaries are dissolving, and players on every platform stand to benefit.
