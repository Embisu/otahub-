Ba Tu The Chien Dau — Nen Tang Cua Moi Tran Danh

Khong nhu nhieu game action RPG, Black Myth: Wukong co ba tu the (Stance) hoan toan khac nhau, moi tu the phu hop voi mot phong cach choi rieng:

Smash Stance: Tu the mac dinh, combo nhanh va linh hoat. Ideal cho nguoi moi vi de hoc nhung kho master. Charged attack cua Smash co the interrupt nhieu boss giua chung combo.

Pillar Stance: Chuyen Thiet Bang thanh cot dai, danh tu xa va co AoE lon. Cuc ky manh voi nhom enemy nho va boss hay giu khoang cach. Ket hop voi Spirit Pillar Stance de gay thiet hai khong lo.

Thrust Stance: Tan cong nhanh theo duong thang, damage cao nhat tren hit don. Phu hop voi nguoi choi thich toc do va choi aggressive.

Vessel — Suc Manh Bi An Tu Binh Bau

Vessel la he thong dac trung nhat cua Black Myth: Wukong. Bang cach thu thap nguyen lieu tu boss va crafting tai Shrine, ban mo khoa cac Vessel mang lai buff cuc manh khi kich hoat. Moi Vessel co dac tinh rieng: Loong Vessel tang crit rate va Spark damage, Azure Dust Vessel cho phep hap thu mau tu enemy bi danh nga, trong khi Supreme Vessel la Vessel manh nhat chi co duoc sau khi hoan thanh True Ending.

Spirit — Bien Ke Thu Thanh Suc Manh

Sau khi danh bai mot so boss va miniboss nhat dinh, ban co the thu thap linh hon (Spirit) cua chung va su dung nhu mot ky nang dac biet. Co hon 30 Spirit khac nhau va viec chon dung Spirit cho tung tinh huong la yeu to quyet dinh: Red Loong Spirit cho burst damage, Wandering Wight Spirit cho tankiness, con Whiteclad Noble Spirit cho healing.

Meo De Vuot Boss Kho

Mot so loi khuyen da duoc kiem chung boi cong dong: Luon giu it nhat 2 Gourd charges (binh thuoc) cho boss fight. Hoc ky animation cua boss truoc khi tan cong — hau het boss co cua so punish ro rang sau combo 3-4 hit. Su dung Immobilize spell (bat dong) khi boss o nguong mau thap de gay them damage tu do. Dung quen nang cap Gourd tai Shrine de tang luong hoi phuc moi lan uong.

Build Khuyen Nghi Cho Nguoi Moi

Neu ban moi bat dau, hay dau tu vao cay ky nang Smash Stance de mo khoa Focus Points nhanh nhat. Uu tien: nang cap Gourd → mo khoa Immobilize → lay Critical Strike tu cay Foundation → sau do tuy theo so thich. Dung spread deu tat ca — dau tu sau vao mot tu the se hieu qua hon nhieu trong giai doan dau game.

Black Myth: Wukong is the most visually stunning and the most surprisingly punishing game of 2024-2025 if you are unprepared. Built by Game Science with difficulty leaning toward challenging experienced players, complete newcomers can still experience it fully if they understand the core mechanics correctly. Here is what you need to know before starting.

Understand the Three Core Stances

Black Myth has 3 Stances for Wukong's Staff: Smash Stance (high damage, high lag), Pillar Stance (fast combos, flexible) and Thrust Stance (medium damage, extended range). The most common beginner mistake is staying too long in Smash Stance for the high damage. In practice, Pillar Stance is the most important Stance early on because it allows animation-cancel after each strike, improving your ability to avoid boss hits rhythmically. Thrust Stance becomes more valuable in mid-game when you face bosses with long AI patterns and difficult close-approach.

Vessel: Choose Guanyin Before Others

Vessels are blessings obtained from Shrines that allow Wukong to manifest certain Guanyin-and-beast combined skills. Vessel of Guanyin should be your first priority because it increases hp recovery on successful hits, reducing the "can't survive" feeling when hit repeatedly without healing. Do not confuse this with general game-wide vessels: Vessels change based on previously defeated boss history and only unlock according to Chapter progress. Not every Vessel is available from Chapter 1.

Transformation: Read Carefully Before Using

Transformation is one of the game's most beautiful features: Wukong transforms into animals taken from defeated bosses, each with their own moveset and stats. The beginner mistake is using any Transformation whenever possible, treating it as free DPS. In reality, each Transformation has optimal conditions: Red Tides Tiger is strongest when clearing small enemy groups, while Wandering Wight is most effective for quickly recovering HP before a boss fight. Understanding each Transformation's strengths and weaknesses helps far more than simply spamming the most powerful one.

First Boss: Tiger Vanguard

Tiger Vanguard is the first boss where many players get stuck. Weak point: the boss's right side (from your perspective). When Tiger Vanguard raises his right foreleg preparing to swing, that is the safe window to attack. Avoid staying directly in front when the boss chases you. Do not use Gourd (the drinking vessel) too early: save it for after you have learned a specific pattern rather than panic-healing. When the Gourd runs empty, the nearest Shrine will refill it.

Spirits: Choose Based on Your Style

Spirits are add-on skill layers that can stack onto any build. Early on, remember one rule: prioritize Spirits with HP regeneration (Critical HP recovery) over Spirits that boost damage. Your damage output is sufficient to get through Chapters 1-2, but if you lose HP in an uncontrolled way, you will be depleted before the final boss. Health sustain always matters more than damage at the very beginning.

Wukong has every tool, but you must be the one to discover when to use each one. The game never tells you directly. Game Science, Dev Commentary — Black Myth: Wukong

Settings to Prioritize Before Playing

Before entering the game: enable Performance Mode on console (stable 60fps matters more than prettier 30fps), turn off Motion Blur and Chromatic Aberration (reduces eye fatigue), increase enemy attack warning Audio cue volume (helps reactive dodging). On PC, update your Nvidia/AMD driver to the latest version as Game Science frequently changes optimization. These changes will not increase your skill but they reduce the random element.
