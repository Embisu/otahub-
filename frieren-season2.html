Summary
Frieren Season 2 do Madhouse (thay Madhouse phan 1) san xuat. 24 tap. Arc chinh: hanh trinh den Aureole va Stille. Nhan vat moi: Wirbel, Sein chinh thuc tro lai. Diem: 9.3/10.
Frieren Season 2 do Madhouse (thay Madhouse phan 1) san xuat. 24 tap. Arc chinh: hanh trinh den Aureole va Stille. Nhan vat moi: Wirbel, Sein chinh thuc tro lai. Diem: 9.3/10.

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End la mot trong nhung anime xuat sac nhat thap nien 2020s — khong phai vi hanh dong, khong phai vi twist, ma vi su sang suot cua no khi tiep can thoi gian va mat mat. Season 2 tiep tuc tu diem Frieren, Fern va Stark chuan bi cho cuoc thi First Class Mage Exam va hanh trinh cuoi cung den Aureole — noi duoc cho la co the noi chuyen voi nguoi chet.

First Class Mage Exam: Thu Thach Lon Nhat Tu Truoc Den Nay

Phan lon Season 2 dien ra trong Boi Thanh — khu vuc nguy hiem noi ky thi First Class Mage duoc to chuc. Frieren can vuot qua ky thi nay de tiep tuc hanh trinh, nhung cuoc thi khong chi danh gia suc manh phap thuat — no danh gia ca tri tue, chien thuat va kha nang lam viec nhom. Day la co hoi Season 2 gioi thieu nhieu mage moi voi phong cach va triet ly rat khac nhau — trong do noi bat nhat la Wirbel, mot mage co qua khu phuc tap va dong co khong ro rang.

Fern va Stark: Phat Trien Nhieu Hon Season 1

Mot trong nhung diem yeu it oi cua Season 1 la Fern va Stark co it screen time de phat trien rieng. Season 2 sua chua dieu nay — ca hai co nhieu episode tap trung vao quan diem ca nhan, ky uc va muc tieu rieng ngoai viec theo chan Frieren. Dac biet, moi quan he giua Fern va Stark trong Season 2 duoc xu ly theo cach tinh te va tu nhien hon nhieu so voi phan lon romance trong anime.

Madhouse Tiep Nhan: Chat Luong Animation Co Duoc Giu Nguyen?

Studio Madhouse thay the Madhouse phan 1 — mot doi thay khien nhieu fan lo lang. Nhung tren thuc te, Madhouse dau tu rat nhieu vao Frieren Season 2: director moi nhung voi cau truc nhan su tuong tu, background art van dat dung chuan phong canh ly tuong, va character animation du nguyen chat luong. Mot so reviewer cho rang mot so canh trong Season 2 con dep hon ca Season 1.

Nhung Moment Lam Dung Lai: Frieren Noi Chuyen Voi Ai?

Dieu lam Frieren khac biet la no khong co khach thu su dung. Frieren dang noi chuyen voi tat ca nhung ai co nguoi than da qua doi, nhung ai cam thay thoi gian tro lai qua nhanh, nhung ai bai khoan ve y nghia cua nhung moi quan he ngan ngui trong mot cuoc doi dai. Moi episode cua Season 2 co it nhat mot moment — mot cau thoai, mot canh nhin, mot bai nhac — khien ban phai dung lai va nghi.

Danh Gia: 9.3/10 — Dinh Cao Cua Slow-Life Fantasy

Frieren Season 2 la tiep noi xung dang cua mot trong nhung anime xuat sac nhat thap nien. No khong la perfect — mot so arc phu cua ky thi hoi dai — nhung tong the no la mot trai nghiem khong the thay the. 9.3/10 cho mot anime khong co ai la villain, khong co twist giat gan, nhung van khong the ngung xem.
