Solo Leveling S2 premieres October 2026. Arcs: Jeju Island + Monarchs Conflict. Director: Shunsuke Nakashige returns. Soundtrack: Hiroyuki Sawano returns. Simulcast on Crunchyroll + Amazon Prime.

Khi Solo Leveling mua 1 ket thuc vao dau nam 2025, no da tro thanh mot trong nhung series duoc xem nhieu nhat trong lich su Crunchyroll va ban dau anime tren Amazon Prime. Nen viec A-1 Pictures xac nhan mua 2 la dieu hoan toan khong co gi phai ngac nhien, nhung nhung gi ho tiet lo ve qui mo san xuat thi chac chan lam nhieu fan xuc dong: Solo Leveling: Arise from the Shadow se ra mat thang 10 nam 2026, va cac key visual da cho thay mot buoc nhay ve chat luong.

Jeju Island Arc: Man Dai Chien Quan Trong Nhat

Mua 2 bat dau tu noi mua 1 con dang, nhung nhanh chong day vao Jeju Island arc, mot trong nhung su kien quan trong nhat trong lich su Solo Leveling. Dao Jeju, voi mat do quai vat khung khiep, la noi cua hanh dong tap the quy mo lon nhat trong manhwa: cac hunters tu nhieu quoc gia hop tac, National Level Hunters tham chien va Sung Jinwoo phai dua ra mot trong nhung quyet dinh kho khan nhat cua minh. Arc nay chuyen doi hanh trinh cua nhan vat chinh tu "nguoi manh hon" thanh "nguoi phai ganh nhac trach nhiem".

Monarchs Conflict: Tinh Huong Chinh Cua Season

Tiep sau Jeju Island la Monarchs Conflict, noi cau truyen bat dau mo rong vuot ra ngoai cai hung cua mot "nguoi ve cuoi danh len". Nha Vua Song cho, Monarch cua Bong Toi, tro nen xung dot voi nhung thuc the co suc manh tuong duong. Arc nay tiet lo nhieu hon ve nguon goc cua Hunter System, ly do ton tai cua Dungeons va moi lien ket giua Jinwoo voi nguoi tien nhiem cua minh. Day la noi Solo Leveling thuc su ly thu: khi worldbuilding duoc trien khai tot va power fantasy co tong su tu biet.

Doi Ngu San Xuat

Shunsuke Nakashige, director cua mua 1, quay tro lai cung doi ngu animation. Diem lon nhat la su tro lai cua Hiroyuki Sawano lam composer, nguoi da tao ra nhac nen co tac dong lon den trai nghiem xem mua 1. A-1 Pictures xac nhan ho tang so tap len 16 tu 12 cua mua 1, cho phep xu ly day du ca Jeju Island lan phan dau cua Monarchs arc. Khong co split-cour.

Gi Moi So Voi Mua 1?

Ngoai noi dung, A-1 Pictures tiet lo mot so thay doi ki thuat: shader moi cho hieu ung Shadow Army se toi hon, co chieu sau hon va it "chibi-ish" hon so voi lan dau hien thi; CGI va 2D se ket hop muan mang hon dac biet trong canh Sung Jinwoo Monarch form; va voice director moi cho phap tu ta len lop tien nang tiem cua Taito Ban trong vai Jinwoo.

Xem O Dau va Khi Nao?

Solo Leveling Season 2 simulcast tren Crunchyroll va Amazon Prime Video, phat song hang tuan bat dau tu thang 10/2026. Lich phat song cu the chua duoc cong bo nhung nhieu kha nang la cuoi tuan, theo xu huong cua mua 1. Viet Nam nam trong vung Crunchyroll Dong Nam A va Amazon Prime Asia.

Jeju Island arc la noi Jinwoo tro nen nguoi anh hung that su, khong chi la nguoi manh nhat. Chung toi muon su khac biet do the hien ro qua animation. Shunsuke Nakashige, Director, A-1 Pictures

When Solo Leveling Season 1 concluded in early 2025, it had become one of the most-watched series in Crunchyroll history and Amazon Prime anime records. So A-1 Pictures confirming Season 2 surprised no one, but what they revealed about production scale certainly moved many fans: Solo Leveling: Arise from the Shadow premieres October 2026, and the key visuals already show a clear quality leap.

Jeju Island Arc: The Most Important Major Battle

Season 2 picks up where Season 1 left off but quickly accelerates into the Jeju Island arc, one of the most consequential events in Solo Leveling's entire history. Jeju Island, with its terrifying monster density, is the setting for the manhwa's largest-scale cooperative action: hunters from multiple nations working together, National Level Hunters entering combat and Sung Jinwoo making one of his hardest decisions yet. This arc transforms the protagonist's journey from "person who gets stronger" to "person who must shoulder responsibility".

Monarchs Conflict: The Season's True Core

Following Jeju Island comes the Monarchs Conflict, where the story expands beyond the framework of one person's power progression. The Shadow Monarch, King of the Dead, enters conflict with entities of comparable power. This arc reveals far more about the Hunter System's origins, the reason Dungeons exist and Jinwoo's connection to his predecessor. This is where Solo Leveling becomes truly interesting: when the worldbuilding matures and the power fantasy acquires genuine narrative purpose.

Production Team

Shunsuke Nakashige, Season 1's director, returns with the same animation team. The big news is Hiroyuki Sawano returning as composer, who created the soundtrack that so dramatically shaped the viewing experience of Season 1. A-1 Pictures confirms an increase to 16 episodes from Season 1's 12, allowing full coverage of both Jeju Island and the opening of the Monarchs arc. No split-cour format.

What's New Compared to Season 1?

Beyond content, A-1 Pictures revealed several technical improvements: new shaders for the Shadow Army effects will be darker, more dimensional and less stylized than their first appearance; CGI and 2D will blend more seamlessly, especially in Sung Jinwoo's Monarch form scenes; and a new voice director will help Taito Ban unlock more of his untapped potential in the Jinwoo role.

Where and When to Watch

Solo Leveling Season 2 simulcasts on Crunchyroll and Amazon Prime Video, airing weekly from October 2026. Specific broadcast day is yet to be announced but will likely be a weekend slot consistent with Season 1. Vietnam is within Crunchyroll's Southeast Asia and Amazon Prime Asia territories.

The Jeju Island arc is where Jinwoo becomes a true hero, not just the strongest person. We want that difference to show clearly through the animation. Shunsuke Nakashige, Director, A-1 Pictures
