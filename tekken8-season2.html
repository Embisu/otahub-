Tekken 8 Season 2 ra mat thang 5/2026. 4 nhan vat moi: Armor King, Lei Wulong, Jun Kazama upgrade, guest character. Cap nhat Heat System: Heat Smash moi cho moi nhan vat. Metacritic patch: 91.
Tekken 8 Season 2 ra mat thang 5/2026. 4 nhan vat moi: Armor King, Lei Wulong, Jun Kazama upgrade, guest character. Cap nhat Heat System: Heat Smash moi cho moi nhan vat. Metacritic patch: 91.

Tekken 8 da co mot nam ra mat thuoc hang kinh dien — doanh thu 2 trieu ban trong 3 thang dau, duoc danh gia la fighting game hay nhat ke tu Tekken 5. Season 2 khong co y dinh nghit tay: Bandai Namco tu tin tuyen bo day la "ban cap nhat lon nhat trong lich su Tekken", va sau khi check list noi dung, tuyen bo nay kho ma phu nhan.

4 Nhan Vat Moi: Nhung Ai Tro Lai?

Armor King cuoi cung duoc xac nhan sau nhieu thang tin don — lugg/wrestler hybrid voi combo potential hang top tier. Lei Wulong, "Jackie Chan cua Tekken", tro lai voi phong cach da stance phuc tap nhat game. Jun Kazama nhan ban cap nhat lon coi nhu la mot nhan vat hoan toan moi voi moveset mo rong va Heat State moi. Va guest character thu 4 — bat ngo — la mot nhan vat tu franchise RPG dinh dam ma Bandai Namco chua chinh thuc cong bo khi bai nay len song.

Cap Nhat Heat System: Moi Nhan Vat Co Heat Smash Rieng

Change lon nhat trong Season 2 la viec Bandai Namco them Heat Smash rieng biet cho moi nhan vat thay vi chia se animation chung. Dieu nay co nghia la moi nhan vat bay gio co mot super move doc dao — Kazuya Electric Heat Smash khac hoan toan voi Bryan Fury Gatling Heat Smash. Day la thay doi can bang lon nhat ke tu launch va da pha dao community: mot so nhan vat len top tier, mot so bi nerf dang ke.

Balance Patch: Top Tier Thay Doi Hoan Toan

Season 2 cung kem theo ban patch balance lon nhat ke tu launch. Victor — nhan vat bi coi la overpowered trong Season 1 — bi nerf dang ke. Ngược lai, Kuma/Panda va Devil Jin duoc buff de co the competitive hon. Community tier list da phai viet lai hoan toan sau patch, va dieu nay tao ra meta moi thu vi hon rat nhieu cho competitive scene.

Road to EVO va Lich Su Kien

Tekken 8 Season 2 ra mat dung dip de chuan bi cho EVO 2026 — giai dau fighting game lon nhat the gioi. Bandai Namco xac nhan Season 2 se la version duoc su dung tai EVO, va ho cam ket khong co major balance patch trong thoi gian preparation. Ngoai EVO con co Tekken World Tour 2026 voi prize pool tang gap doi so voi 2025.

Danh Gia: 9.0/10 — Fighting Game Hay Nhat Hien Tai

Season 2 khong chi la noi dung bo sung — no la mot ban phuc sinh cho toan bo meta cua Tekken 8. Voi 4 nhan vat moi, Heat System cai tien va balance patch toan dien, Tekken 8 co the tu tin giu vi tri fighting game hay nhat thi truong trong it nhat mot nam nua. 9.0/10.
